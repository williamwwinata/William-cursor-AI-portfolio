**Last updated**: 2026-04-14  
**Status**: Draft — awaiting user review  
**Goal**: Identify 15+ practitioners who have run real webinar funnels and share verifiable results. Final list will be 6-10 after review.

**Quality gates:**
- **P** = Practitioner (cites real numbers: conversion %, revenue, attendee counts)
- **R** = Recency (active content in 2023–2025)
- **D** = Depth (3+ pieces of content on webinar funnels on their platform)
- **T** = Teachability (explains the "how," not just self-promotion)
