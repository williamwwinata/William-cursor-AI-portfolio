I’m realizing that the toughest thing in selling a blue ocean product is that our ideal customers don’t know they live in pain.

They’ve normalized their pain as the status quo. “It’s just the way things are.” "Everyone does the same thing."

People don't see revenue "not gained" as "revenue lost". That's their biggest problem. And ours!

All these years we’ve been selling the benefits of our product, when we should be helping people see their **cost of inaction.**

I've been quiet because I've been living under a rock trying to figure out how to get eWebinar back into growth mode.

I finally understand that our biggest opportunity isn't chasing the tiny slice of "innovators" who already get it. It's unlocking the much larger audience behind them by helping them see what they're missing.

I'm in the process of unlocking true PMF, and I’ll be sharing every success and failure along the way.
