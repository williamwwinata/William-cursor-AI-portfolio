On my upcoming newsletter on Sunday, I'm going to tell the story of what it was like to pitch ~20 VCs with my mediocre, non-venture scalable idea and how we found product-market fit by getting rejected over and over. 🙌 

I'll share all the the sh*t VCs have said to me which I'm sure you've also heard...from "we invest in companies at all stages" (they don't), and "I'd love to stay in touch" (high chance they didn't care).

I'll also share all the fringe benefits I experienced from trying to raise capital and failing. It's a great way to get to know your business and yourself in a very short amount of time so you can choose the right path to keep going down.

💰 Many people think funding is a financial decision, when funding is a lifestyle choice.

What’s my newsletter about?
It’s a bi-weekly column with relatable stories of my journey bootstrapping 3 companies. No BS, just straight-up truth bombs on what it’s like to build a company without an abundance of resources or friends in high places.
