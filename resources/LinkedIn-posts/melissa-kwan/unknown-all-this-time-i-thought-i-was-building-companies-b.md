All this time, I thought I was building companies, but I was really building myself. 🤔 

Building startups taught me the value of money by showing me how hard it is to earn.

It taught me persistence, resilience, and patience. It taught me about people, empathy, and compassion.

It revealed sides of me that I never knew existed. It forced me to grow, not because I wanted to, but because I had to in order to survive.

They say, "It's not personal, it's just business." 

But business is personal, and I'm glad it is.
