On our way to $1m ARR, we spent tried a bunch of go-to-market strartegies that didn't work. Here are the top 10: 👇  

1. Product Hunt
Put in way too much time and effort, got lots of exposure but 1 known customer.

2. Digital marketing
We hired a number of agencies to "do digital marketing", ended up needing to own it ourselves.

3. Paid ads
We didn't have enough budget to do this meaningfully and our product is not a scroll stopper.

4. Writing mediocre content
Ended up with low qualiity content even I couldn't finish reading that didn't convert.

5. Email outreach
Does anyone read cold emails anymore? Maybe for enterprise products, but not ours.

6. LinkedIn outreach
People HATED this, it's the cringiest thing I've ever done. Please don't try this at home.

7. Affiliates and partnerships
We were too early to invest in this as a primary channel.

8. Event sponsorships
Huge waste of money. I already kind of knew, but wanted to be sure.

9. Newsletters
Didn't matter how big the list was, clickthrough rate was very slim.

10. Big design update
Had hopes this would dazzle more people and increase conversion. it didn't.

There were a lot of things we thought would move the needle, but they didn't.

Everything that didn't work cost a lot of money. Everything that worked didn't cost money, but require a lot of time. Another reminder that there are no short cuts and you can't pay someone else to take away your pain.
