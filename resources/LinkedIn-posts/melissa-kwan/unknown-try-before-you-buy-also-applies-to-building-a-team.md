'Try before you buy' also applies to building a team. You don't need to jump into it right away. ⛔ 

Start with contractors and see how well you work together before committing on both sides.

Even our Cofounder/CTO was a part-time contractor for months before we decided to work on eWebinar together. And he's my husband! 😂 

During that time, he grew to love the product, and I realized I wouldn't be able to build this startup without him. That was a year after founding the company.
