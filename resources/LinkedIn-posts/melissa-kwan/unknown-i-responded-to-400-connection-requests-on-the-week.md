I responded to 400+ connection requests on the weekend, then got a notice from LinkedIn that they detected I could be using an automation tool.

I've gotten temporarily banned here a few times before, once permanently banned, all without an explanation because their T&C states that they do not have to tell you the reason. The last time this happened, I was on a viral streak and had to go through my network to find someone who worked in the company to reinstate my account. 

When this happens to you, there's no support person you can contact and no recourse; there's nothing you can say because they don't give you a reason to speak up against. Nor, do they care. You are simply 1 out of a billion users.

This was the primary reason I started diversifying into other platforms, and started a newsletter so I can connect with people who opt-into receiving my content.

These notices are precursors to potentially being permanently locked out of your account, after years of building an audience on here. I can't tell you now demoralizing that is.

If you think you own your audience on here, think again.

The biggest mistake I made was not to funnel my audience from here into Substack when I hit 10 viral posts in a row two years ago. All of those posts were getting 500k-1m impressions. Every creator was telling me to do that, but I procrastinated until I lost that streak.

If the algo likes you right now, the best thing you can do for yourself is repurpose that content into newsletters, and get people's email.

Email is still king, and there's nothing like landing in someone's Inbox who actually want to hear from you. You don't own your audience unless you have direct access to them. Period.

You cannot build your business on rented land. Do not make the same mistake as I did. 

It takes half a day to set up a Substack account (or whatever you decide to use). Don't wait.

( 💌 PS. You gotta love that I'm getting slapped on the wrist for using LI legitimately, while the hundreds of automated sales messages I get per day are still going strong. Who is making decisions here?)
