To kick off 2026, I wanted to properly reintroduce myself. 👋 I've spent the last three years sharing pieces of my founder journey, but I've never told you the complete story from the first moment at 5 years old when I decided to build something in the back of a taxi, to last year when I burned out so badly I couldn't remember why I started any of this.

Here it is: 15 years, three companies, one acquisition, and how I became who I am now.

→ I started my career in real estate

→ Then manufactured furniture in China

→ Got into tech via SAP, but wasn't made for corporate

→ Started my first company which turned into an agency

→ Started my second company, sold it after 4 hard years

→ Started my third company 2 months later, was going well

→ Ended up burning out hard last year, went on a life journey

→ Wanted to give up, confronted myself, untangled some deep sh*t

→ Realized why I wasn't ready for the success I've always wanted...until now

I've finally arrived. 🙌
