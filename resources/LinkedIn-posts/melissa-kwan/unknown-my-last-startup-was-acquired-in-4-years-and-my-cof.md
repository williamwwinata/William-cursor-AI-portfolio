My last startup was acquired in 4 years and my cofounder and I owned 94%. It took 2.5 years before we made our first $10, then just a year to break even.

Nothing was easy about that company...from finding product market fit with a blue ocean product, to grueling and long sales cycles, to low contract values.

That was my first product company (previous company to that was an agency). The hardest thing about it was that we had no experience. Everything had to be learned from scratch. There were endless trials and tribulations, failures more than successes.

💡 However, there were pivotal 10 aha! moments that got us to acquisition 1.5 years after we made our first dollar.

📰 On my newsletter this Sunday, I'm going to share what those aha! moments were in detail, and how each one contributed to the growth of my business and myself as an entrepreneur.

Without powering through those years, I would not be able to start eWebinar 2 months after with the knowledge I had, and generate revenue from launch day.
