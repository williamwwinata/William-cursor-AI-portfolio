I've been thinking a lot about what product-market fit is, and whether it's a myth. I've rarely seen companies where customers "rush to sign up." 

Most great businesses I personally know of, founders have built over time and still require salespeople with long sales cycles, especially for enterprise.

What I do know is that PMF doesn't happen at $1m ARR, that is not the magic number, contrary to what you read.

If you have PMF, I'm curious to know your story if you're open to sharing. How did you find it? How long did it take? What happened?!

For those who have an opinion, what is and what isn't PMF? Comment below! 👇
