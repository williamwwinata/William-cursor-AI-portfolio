Most founders have no idea how to do financial projections. But without it, it's  hard to understand how your decisions impact cashflow.

A friend taught me how to do this when I started my first business 14 years ago, and now it's a document I can't live without. It gives me clarity into the levers that drive our business at least 12 months out. 

I obsessively update it with actual numbers every 1st of the month.

💰 Projections allow me to answer questions like:
• Can we afford to hire this person?
• When will the business break even?
• Which subscription is driving the most revenue?
• Where will we be by the end of this year, and next?
• What does our growth rate have to be each month?

Lauren Pearl, CFO advisor, took the time to make a video review of my projections template and made suggestions on improvements you can make.

I converted my easy-to-follow SaaS financial projections into a sharable template you can duplicate and make your own.
