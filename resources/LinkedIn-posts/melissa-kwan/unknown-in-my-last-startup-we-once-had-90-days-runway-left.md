In my last startup, we once had 90-days runway left. I promised myself to leave no stone unturned.

I did everything in my power to make the business go before we had no choice but to shut down.

I reached out to every potential prospect that might buy our half-baked product, every person that could have money to invest, and took every call even if it felt like waste of time.

With that determination, a private investor who found us through the App Store wrote us a check that helped us pay off our debts, bring on one more developer, and propel us forward.

2 years later, that company was acquired.

Every founder has a never-quit story. What's yours ⁉️
