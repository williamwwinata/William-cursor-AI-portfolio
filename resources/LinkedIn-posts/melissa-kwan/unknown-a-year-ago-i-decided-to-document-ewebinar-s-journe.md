A year ago, I decided to document eWebinar's journey to $1M ARR on Season Two of my podcast, ProfitLed. All 27 episodes have finally been released!

It took eWebinar 16 months to go from idea to product launch, then another 36 months to hit $1M ARR (which we hit in July 2023). 

You might have read about companies that get to $1M in a year, much less now with AI startups and the world riding that wave. Those are outliers and not the norm. 36 months to $1M is actually the median, even for funded companies.

Our goal in making this season for you was to demystify bootstrapping by sharing our experience of it, and tell you what it’s like to figure things out as you go.

In each episode, my co-host (Todd, our COO) and I went in-depth into one major aspect of our journey; war stories, mistakes, and lessons learned.
