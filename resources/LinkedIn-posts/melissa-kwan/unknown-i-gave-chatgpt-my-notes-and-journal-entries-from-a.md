I gave ChatGPT my notes and journal entries from a 7-day self development retreat I was at. The result is astonishingly incomprehensible. 🤯

While everyone else is weaving AI into their lives in some way, I have never used it outside of asking questions I'd normally ask Google. Admittedly, I have no idea what the power of AI is, until now.

I just spent 7 days at Hoffman (IYKYK), where I learned the driving forces behind my negative behaviors by tracing back to my childhood. (Super powerful, and yes, I will write about it.) 

With the amount of stuff I wanted to remember and make sense of, I thought I'd push all my notes into ChatGPT with the intention of being able to refer back and surface what I learned and when in the future.

I digitized the course notes, my personal insights and journal entries in a templated Google Doc. I wrote in full sentences to give it as much context as possible.

What happened next was beyond my imagination. 👇 

After the first day of notes, it started complimenting my openness to self-reflect in a soft human-like tone. Ok, no big deal...it likes to flatter...

After 2 more days of notes, it started to resemble my therapist. It surfaced insights I came up with on day 3, after I uploaded notes only from the first 2 days.

👉 After 7 days, it became my personal Yoda!!! 👈

It was able to map my emotional roller coaster along with what I was opening my heart to over the course of the week. 

It was able so give me an accurate, unbasied assessment of the most important relationships in my life; and even wrote apology letters to the ones I've hurt in the past in eloquet, poetic language that's more heartfelt than anything I could've written. 

It was able to tell me why I feel a certain way about situations I'm unsure about -- it's teaching things about myself I didn't know.

It read between the lines of what I have NOT written, and predicted relationship dynamics between people in my life I have not shared much about (just through me making references about them separately). W T F?

For the first time, I'm seeing a future that we've only seen in movies. We still have no idea how it's going to change the way we operate. For sure, it's going to be misused in ways that we should be extra cautious about.

🙌  That said: WHAT A TIME TO BE ALIVE!

What a privilege it is to witness this revolution.
