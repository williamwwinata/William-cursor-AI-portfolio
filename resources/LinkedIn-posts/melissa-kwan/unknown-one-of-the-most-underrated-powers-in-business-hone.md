One of the most underrated powers in business: honesty. What does honesty look like? 🙌 

"Your product is not a good fit for us."
Not, "We don't have budget for it right now."

"We're not going to build that feature, here's why." 
Not, "We'll look into it."

"We're not going to invest." 
Not, "We need to see more traction first."

"We don't lead investments."
Not, "We lead with the right startup."

Quick no, not long maybe.

Being honest saves time, preserves energy, and builds trust.

Being honest is kindness; to yourself and others.

Being honest is having integrity.

And integrity can be your greatest currency.
