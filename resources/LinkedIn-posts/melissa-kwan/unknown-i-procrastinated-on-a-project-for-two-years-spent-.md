I procrastinated on a project for two years. Spent two weeks with Claude and got it done. 😳 

I'm both mind blown and terrified at the same time...

I was tasked to rewrite 33 product emails. They’re onboarding, activation, and nurturing emails that every customer sees when they sign up for an eWebinar trial, convert to a paying subscriber, and/or hit the cancel button ☹️.

The last time I did this was 4.5 years ago when we first launched. It took 4 months with a product marketing consultant to complete. I’m not a product marketer, so this kind of writing is mentally draining and sucks the life out of me.

The emails in our product no longer matched our offering. They were all over the place and didn’t sound like our brand voice.

I've been dragging my feet on this because the thought of going through that process again felt harder than climbing Mount Everest. It’s one of those things you start doing…then realize you need to go organize your sock drawer. 🧦 

A friend told me to check out Claude, said it was great for writing content. I gave it a shot a couple weeks ago. I kept about 50% of the elements from the original emails, and updated the rest. I’m astonished by the result, and how quickly and accurately it was able to communicate everything I wanted it to, in a conversational tone that sounds like me.

Without AI, this would’ve taken me a year. Probably longer. I might have even quit my own startup to keep avoiding the task. That’s how much I didn't want to do it.

I procrastinated for so long that AI was invented. That’s epic. 🤩
