When you only have 3 months of runway left, is when you need to do something significantly different.

A friend once told me this when I was struggling to find product-market fit in my last company after trying for 2 years.

I followed his advice, stripped our overbuilt product down to a single feature, then made our first $10 within a week.

A year later, we broke even. Another year later, that startup was acquired.
