💥 On Monday, I'm dropping my latest newsletter article: What losing delusional self-belief did to my startup

I built three companies with sheer conviction that I knew what customers wanted more than feedback they're able to give. No customer interviews, no traditional bare-bones MVPs.

But...somewhere along the way, 1.5 years ago, I stopped believing in myself. I started second guessing every decision, thinking my startup had outgrown me.

Our growth started plateauing, and so did I.

I stopped trusting myself, and my startup mirrored that silence.

❤️‍🔥  This is a story fear, fire, and finding my way back to myself.

It’s raw. It’s uncomfortable. It's the most honest thing I’ve written about my human journey behind building a startup…the life that happens in the pursuit of freedom.

If you’ve ever felt like you were the one holding your startup back, this one's for you.
