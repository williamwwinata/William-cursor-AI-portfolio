Our competitor signed up an account with us (using their work email 😂), what did we do?!

Nothing.

Companies are often scared their competitors will see what they're up to, leading them to gate their product and pricing.

Your competitors already know about you, as you know about them.

We don't worry about our competition. We focus on winning customers that value our product above others.

💡 They can copy where we are, but not where we're headed.
