The quickest way to lose consumer trust and confidence? Lie to them. 🤥 

I saw an interesting AI video generation company I wanted to try. Sign up says "no credit card required."

I login, there are 20 free credits. I put some prompts in, hit publish, it makes me choose a plan and put in a credit card before it'll move forward.

The credit card isn't the problem. The deception is. This is called a bait and switch. 

I feel tricked, I bounce.

Everyone has to make money. We get it. But please, just call things what they are.

If you need a card, say "card is required and you can cancel any time."

Stop putting "contact sales" on every subscription plan type.

Don't say "see demo now" and pop up a contact form.

Got limitations on your trial? List them.

Don't default to annual plan.

Treat your prospects as you want to be treated. It's not rocket science.
