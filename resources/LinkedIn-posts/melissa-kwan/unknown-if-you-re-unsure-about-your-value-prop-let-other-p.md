If you’re unsure about your value prop, let other people tell you why you’re awesome.

My last startup was an open house check-in solution. It seemed too simple and I had no idea what else it could grow into. 

I met with over 40 industry veterans and asked them where they thought the greatest impact of open houses could be and how our product could be applied. 

After those conversations, I had clear vision of what we needed to be (real-time foot traffic data) and devised a plan on getting there.

We never fully got there before we were acquired, but our roadmap was designed to head in that direction.
