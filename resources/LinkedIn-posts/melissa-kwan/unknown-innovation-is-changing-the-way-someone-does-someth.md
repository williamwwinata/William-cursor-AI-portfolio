Innovation is changing the way someone does something forever. Often, that change is incremental. 🤓 

Change happens with innovation when consumers are ready to adopt, not when you're ready to give it to them.

Everyone thinks innovation has to be a giant leap for mankind. 

But, change that consumers can't understand is often too big of a leap for them to adopt a product.

I've experienced this time and time again through launching products and features that didn't take off because it required users to almost completely change their behavior.
