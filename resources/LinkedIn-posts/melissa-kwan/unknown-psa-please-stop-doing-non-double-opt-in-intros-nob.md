📢  PSA - Please stop doing non-double-opt-in intros. Nobody benefits from those.

1. The person you are making the intro to doesn't want to respond without context and is now annoyed at you.

2. The person you're introducing won't get a response as a result, and is embarrassed for being blindly pushed to a stranger.

3. You look intrusive, disrespectful, and unprofessional for not asking for consent and giving your friend a choice to accept/deny your ask for their time.

Not sure what a double-opt-in?

🤝 The only way to make an intro that builds trust and credibility, and avoids awkward and unwanted connections.
