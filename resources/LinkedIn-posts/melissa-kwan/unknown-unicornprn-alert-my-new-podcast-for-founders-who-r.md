🦄 UnicornPrn alert! My new podcast for founders who roll their eyes at phrases like “we’re killing it" and "hockey stick growth" is live. Episode list below👇

A few years ago, my co-host, Lloyed Lobo and I were going through professional struggles and turned to each other for founder therapy.

As we grew closer as friends, our conversations got more real, more raw, and more hilarious. We’d riff about investor horror stories, founder mistakes, and bloated valuations. Things we experienced ourselves, heard from our friends, or read about in the media. We started joking about how great it would be if we could make our conversations public. 

There’s so much smoke and mirrors in tech especially when founders have to keep up appearances for investors. Those glorified stories aren’t always an accurate representation of the truth. It’s what Lloyed started calling, “Unicorn P0rn”.

When I heard those two words, I thought: That's our show! 🙌  After months of planning, we're finally launching it today.

We talk about all the things you want to say out loud, but can't, or won't...all the nonsense that gives so many founders unrealistic expectations, a twisted definition of success, and lead them to make poor decisions in work and in life.

Our mission is to open the kimono of the startup scene. To give you a different and hopefully more realistic perspective of what happens when you chase the unicorn.

⚠️ Warning: Our unfiltered convos are highly opinionated, brutally honest, and politically incorrect. Don’t tune in if you’re easily offended.
