Your biggest competitive advantage is the LOVE you put into building your company. 💜 What does that mean?

Love is the detail you put into every pixel, every feature.

It's going the extra mile for customers.

It’s being responsive after hours when something breaks.

It's laser-focused execution and follow through, delivering exactly what you said you would, when you would, and taking accountability for shortcomings.

Love isn’t the good work you do because it’s required. It's the great work you do because you want to.

Many companies can do good work, but few are willing to push through the last 2% to make it great. It's too much effort, too time consuming, too little ROI. It doesn't make financial sense.

Great work is exceptional. It can't always be explained, but it can always be felt. It becomes ingrained in your culture. It's how you behave, how you make decisions, how you show up.

Great work is what trust, credibility, and reputation are built on.

Great work is the love. And love is the thing that can't be replicated.

If you keep doing great work, it's impossible for your product not to be good, impossible for customers not to notice, and impossible not to close deals.

Keep doing great work. Great work is love in action.
