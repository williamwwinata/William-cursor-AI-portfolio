I still remember the early days of ClickFunnels like they were yesterday…

Working late into the night, trying to get the first funnel live.
Whiteboarding ideas with the team, hoping this one would be the one that worked.
Chasing that first win, not $1M, just proof that the idea was real.
We didn’t have a massive audience.
 
We didn’t have funding.
We had a vision… and just enough momentum to keep going.
Looking back now, those were the most defining moments.
Not because everything was perfect but because we showed up anyway.

Grateful for every lesson, late night, and leap of faith that brought us here.
And if you're still in those early stages chasing your first win, keep going.
