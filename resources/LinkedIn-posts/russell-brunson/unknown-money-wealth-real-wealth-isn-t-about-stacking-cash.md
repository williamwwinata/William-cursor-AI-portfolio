Money ≠ Wealth

Real wealth isn’t about stacking cash.
It’s about becoming someone who can solve problems, for other people.
Most beginners struggle with money because they’re trying to fix their own situation…
 nstead of solving real, painful problems for someone else.
Once that reframe clicks?
Everything changes.

You stop hoping someone buys.
And you start serving people so well… that buying becomes the natural next step.
Wealth flows to those who serve at scale.
