Entrepreneurship is lonely at the start.
It’s just you.
Your brain.
And a vision nobody else can see yet.

You’ll second guess yourself.
You’ll wonder if you’re crazy.
You’ll hear, “Why don’t you just get a normal job?”

But that’s the price of building something only you can see.

And then, one day, the vision gets clearer.
The team starts forming.
The wins start stacking.
The people who didn’t “get it” start asking how you did it.

But make no mistake…

The early days are meant to feel lonely.
That’s what makes the journey real.
That’s what makes the success meaningful.

So if you're in that phase right now, keep building.
You're not behind. You're just early.
