Most entrepreneurs are working way too hard… and not because they’re lazy but because they’re trying to invent everything from scratch.

You don’t need to reinvent the wheel. You need to find a wheel that rolls… and then make it yours.

🚫 Modeling ≠ Copying Copying is lazy and unethical. Modeling is smart and strategic.

When I launched my first book funnel, I didn’t just guess. 
I studied dozens of funnels from people who were crushing it. 
What pages were they using? 
What did their upsells look like? 
What types of copy did they use?
I didn’t copy one… I modeled the patterns I saw across many. Then I combined those insights with my own voice and value.
The result? A funnel that’s done 1M+ copies (and counting).

Stop trying to build something no one’s ever seen. 
Go study the people already winning in your market. 
Then model what’s working through your lens, with your message.

It’s not less creative, it’s more effective.
