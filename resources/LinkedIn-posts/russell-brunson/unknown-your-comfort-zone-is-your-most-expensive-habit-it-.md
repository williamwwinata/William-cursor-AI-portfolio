Your comfort zone is your most expensive habit.

It costs you:
→ The opportunities you never pursue
→ The clients you never reach
→ The skills you never develop
→ The impact you never make

It doesn’t charge you all at once.

Growth always comes with discomfort.
That first launch.
That first sales call.
That first time you show up online and share your story.
Uncomfortable? Yes.
Worth it? Every time.

If you’re stuck, don’t ask what more you need.
Ask what comfort you’re unwilling to give up.
