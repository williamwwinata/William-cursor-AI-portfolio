Getting 1% better every day… that was always the plan.

Before ClickFunnels.
Before the books.
Before the stage.

It was just me, the mat, and the grind.
I wrestled at BYU until the program was cut due to Title IX.
Transferred to Boise State and finished out my last 4 years of college wrestling.

This photo is a throwback from that season of life.
But the truth is, the lessons I learned on the mat still shape how I show up in business today:
✅ Discipline > motivation
✅ Obsession creates momentum
✅ Small improvements compound
✅ Wins are earned long before the spotlight hits

Entrepreneurship isn’t that different from wrestling.
You show up.
You study your opponent.
You get knocked down.
And then you decide to keep going.

Every day. Every rep. Every round.
