Most marketers tell the epiphany.
Great marketers?
They make you feel it.

A great story doesn’t just explain a breakthrough…
It recreates the moment of realization for the listener.

Why does that matter?

Because people don’t buy through logic.
They buy through belief.
And belief is built through emotionally felt experiences.

If your story can shift someone’s belief about what’s possible for them sales become effortless.

Story changes belief.
Belief drives action.
That’s when selling feels like serving.

Stop trying to convince.
Start making them feel.
