Everyone shows up on the good days.

The real test?
Showing up when you don’t feel like it.

📉 When the motivation is gone.
📉 When the results aren’t coming.
📉 When quitting feels easier than continuing.

That’s the line between staying stuck and moving forward.

Consistency isn’t built in moments of excitement.
It’s built in the moments of resistance.

The secret to getting ahead?
Show up on the days you want to quit.
That’s when your future self is watching.
