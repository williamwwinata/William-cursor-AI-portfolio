Before ClickFunnels, before the books, before the big stages...
It was just me, a borrowed mic, and a room full of strangers.

In the early days, I learned to sell by doing it the hard way:
Pay your own way to a random hotel event
Hope the audience showed up
Compete with 10 other speakers trying to sell to the same room
And if you didn’t close… you went home empty-handed
That’s how I learned the skill of selling.

Not just the script, but how to read people.
To frame, to persuade, to connect.
To win the room.
Some days I nailed it.
Some days I bombed.
But every rep was sharpening a skill I’d use for the rest of my life.
