I've reviewed thousands of business plans over the last 20 years.
Almost every single one had the same fatal flaw.
They started with the product.
The businesses that actually made it started with a conversation.
Not a product. Not a pitch. Not a business plan.
A single conversation with a single person who had a single problem they desperately needed solved.
Then they built the shortest possible path between that person and that solution.
That's it. That's the whole secret.
The most sophisticated marketing strategies I've ever seen are just very expensive ways to recreate what a great conversation does in 5 minutes.
Before you build another product, find the person.
Before you write another ad, have the conversation.
Before you hire another team member, validate the problem.
The market doesn't reward complexity.
It rewards clarity.
