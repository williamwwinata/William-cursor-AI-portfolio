Ever wonder why you can’t stop watching certain videos?
It’s not just the hook.
It’s the open loop.

In this conversation with Adley Kinsman, we broke down how the most engaging content holds attention…
Not by giving answers upfront, but by creating a gap.

It can be:
– In headlines that hint but does not reveal
– In stories that build curiosity step by step
– In videos where the payoff comes at the end

We learned it by watching every second of retention equal more revenue.
The better we got at opening (and closing) loops, the longer people stayed…
The deeper they engaged…
And the more impact our message made.
