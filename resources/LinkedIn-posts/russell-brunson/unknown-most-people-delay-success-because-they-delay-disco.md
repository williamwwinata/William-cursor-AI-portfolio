Most people delay success…
Because they delay discomfort.

They wait until:
 ➡️ Things are “less busy”
 ➡️ They feel “more confident”
 ➡️ The timing feels “just right”

But growth never shows up wrapped in comfort.
It’s hidden inside the reps you don’t want to do,
The message you’re scared to post,
The decision you keep avoiding.
The longer you avoid discomfort,
The longer you delay your next level.

Success isn’t a mystery.
It’s just uncomfortable.
