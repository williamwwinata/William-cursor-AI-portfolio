Your superpower rarely feels special to you.

Why?

Because it’s easy for you.

The things you do naturally…
The patterns you see instantly…
The problems you can solve in minutes…

You assume everyone else can do them too.

But they can’t.

What feels obvious to you is often invaluable to someone else.

That’s why so many entrepreneurs miss their opportunity at first.
They’re searching for a genius idea…
When their real advantage is the skill they’ve already developed over years of experience.

Your superpower isn’t supposed to feel magical.

It’s supposed to feel normal to you.

The key is learning to see it through someone else’s eyes.

Because once you recognize it…
You can start building something powerful around it.
