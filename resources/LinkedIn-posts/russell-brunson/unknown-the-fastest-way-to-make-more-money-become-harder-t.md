The fastest way to make more money?
Become harder to replace.

Money flows to people who do one thing:

Solve deep, painful, expensive problems… in a way nobody else can.

The most valuable people in any company, business, or industry?
They’re the ones who:
• See around corners
• Make complex things simple
• Create clarity when others are stuck
• Bring strategy and execution to the table
• Build systems that keep working long after they log off

They don’t wait to be told what to do.
They create solutions worth paying for.

So if you want to make more money in 2026, try this:
- Stop trying to look impressive
- Start trying to be indispensable

Learn the problems your dream clients or company obsess over…
Then quietly become the best in the world at solving them.

That’s the game.

Not louder.
Not busier.
Not trendier.

Just harder to replace.
