Your story is your most powerful marketing tool.
Not your credentials.
Not your features.
Not your resume.
Your story.

People don’t buy products.
They buy into people.
They buy into transformation.
They buy into the journey that mirrors their own.

Credentials might impress.
Features might inform.
But your story?
It moves people.

Tell the story that got you to this point.
The struggle.
The lesson.
The breakthrough.

Because that’s what people will remember and respond to.
