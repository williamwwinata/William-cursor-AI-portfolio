Everyone wants the secret to growth.
But most won’t do what actually works.

Here’s how I grew ClickFunnels in the early days:
1) I did a webinar a week for a year.
2) After each one, I exported the chat.
3) I studied every comment, every objection.
4) Then I updated the slides and did it again.
5) Over. And over. And over.

Week after week, until the webinar was so dialed in, I could deliver it from memory.
Most people are looking for a shortcut.
But mastery comes from reps.
And the fastest path to a 7-figure business might just be showing up every week with one great offer and a commitment to get better.

If you want to build momentum, build consistency first.
