Winning doesn’t start on game day.
It starts way before the match ever begins.

It starts with obsession.
With deciding who you’re going to become...
…and then training like it’s already true.

Before anyone cheers for you…
Before anyone believes in you…
You put in the reps.
Not once. But every day. 
Quietly. Consistently.
 
That’s the real game.
The rest is just execution.
