Back when I was wrestling, I watched a VHS tape every day of Tom Brands saying they wanted to be the greatest wrestlers in the world.

So I started saying it too.
Before practice.
Before matches.
On repeat.
It trained my identity.
It locked in my goal.
And it gave everything I did a why.

That same mindset carried over when I stepped into the business world.
I didn’t just “try marketing.”
I became a student of the game.
I read every book. Watched every video. Obsessed over the greats.

Because whether it’s wrestling or funnel, the people who rise are the ones who decide to go deep.

Mastery doesn’t come from dipping your toe in.
It comes from declaring your identity and then backing it up every day.
