Next time something challenges you…

Don’t rush to defend your current belief.
Don’t double down on what you’ve always done.
Don’t let ego build the wall.

Instead…
Ask what it’s trying to teach you.
Because every challenge is a mirror.
And every discomfort is a doorway.

Growth rarely shows up as agreement.
It usually shows up as tension. Resistance. Friction.

The entrepreneurs who grow the fastest aren’t the ones with the right answers.
They’re the ones asking better questions.
Let your next challenge teach you, not trigger you.
