Your story is the strategy.

Not your degrees.
Not your certifications.
Not your features.
Your story.

Why?
Because stories connect.
They build trust faster than a perfectly written sales page.
They create belief in a way bullet points never can.
And they give people a reason to care.

Your journey, the highs, the lows, the lessons, is what turns you from just another marketer into a magnet for the right audience.

If you’re struggling to stand out…
Don’t add more features.
Start telling your story.
That’s what people buy into.
