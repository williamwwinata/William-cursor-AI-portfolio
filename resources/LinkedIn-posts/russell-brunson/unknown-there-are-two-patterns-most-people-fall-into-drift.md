There are two patterns most people fall into.
Drifting…
or Driving.

Drifting is unconscious.
It’s running on autopilot.
It’s waking up, repeating habits you never consciously chose,
believing stories you never questioned,
staying at a “good enough”.

Driven is conscious.
It’s choosing your values.
Defining your identity.

Identity works like a thermostat.
If you don’t consciously raise it,
you snap back to “good.”

And good is the enemy of great.

The real question isn’t
“Are you driven?”

It’s…
Are you consciously designing your life or running on default?
