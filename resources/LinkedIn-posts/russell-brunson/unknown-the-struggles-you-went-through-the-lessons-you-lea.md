The struggles you went through…
The lessons you learned…
The mistakes you made…

That journey is often the foundation of your business.

A lot of people think entrepreneurship starts with a brilliant idea.

But more often, it starts with a problem you once had to solve for yourself.

The late nights figuring things out.
The failures that forced you to learn.
The moments when you wished someone had shown you the way.

Those experiences build something valuable: perspective.

And somewhere out there is someone who’s exactly where you used to be searching for answers you’ve already found.

When you turn your journey into a roadmap for others, something powerful happens.

Your past struggles stop being just memories…
They become the blueprint for impact.

Sometimes the business you’re meant to build is simply helping the person you used to be.
