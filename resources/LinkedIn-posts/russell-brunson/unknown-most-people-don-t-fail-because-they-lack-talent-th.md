Most people don’t fail because they lack talent.
They fail because they leave the door open.
Open to quitting.
Open to going back.
Open to the “safe” version of their life.

But the word decide literally means to cut off.
To remove alternatives.
To eliminate retreat.

If going back is still an option…
Fear will use it.
The shift from drifter to driven doesn’t happen when you feel confident.
It happens when you decide there’s no other path.
No hiding.
No numbing.
No retreat to the ordinary world.

Just forward.
If you want different results, you don’t need more motivation.
You need a decision that closes every exit behind you.
That’s when things change.
