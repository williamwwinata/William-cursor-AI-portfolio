Entrepreneurship isn’t about comfort.

It’s about chasing a vision big enough to scare the old version of you.

The version of you that wanted certainty.
The version that needed approval.
The version that avoided risk.

Because the moment you decide to build something meaningful…
You outgrow the person you used to be.

Bigger vision → bigger decisions.
Bigger decisions → bigger responsibility.
And eventually… a bigger version of you.

That’s the real journey of entrepreneurship.

Not just building a business.
But becoming someone capable of carrying the vision.

If your goals don’t make you a little uncomfortable…
they probably aren’t big enough yet.
