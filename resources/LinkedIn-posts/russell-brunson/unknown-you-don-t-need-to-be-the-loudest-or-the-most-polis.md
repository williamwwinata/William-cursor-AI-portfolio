You don’t need to be the loudest.
Or the most polished.
Or the most “viral.”

You need consistency.

Show up and talk about what you're building.
Even when it feels repetitive.
Even when no one is clapping.
Even when you're not sure it's working.

Because visibility builds trust.
And trust builds momentum.

Every. Single. Day.
That’s how you grow.
