Growth rarely asks, “Are you comfortable?”

It asks, “Are you willing?”

Willing to try before you feel ready.
Willing to look inexperienced while you’re learning.
Willing to keep going when the results aren’t immediate.

Most people wait for the moment when things feel easier, clearer, safer.

But growth doesn’t negotiate with comfort.
It rewards commitment.

The entrepreneurs who move forward aren’t the ones who always feel confident.
They’re the ones who decide the vision is worth the discomfort.

So the real question isn’t:
“Do I feel ready?”

It’s:
“Am I willing to grow into the person this next level requires?"
