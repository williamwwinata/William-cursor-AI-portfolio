If you’re feeling stuck and wondering where to start… start with this question:

What’s your superpower?

Every person has one.

The problem is we usually overlook it… because the things that come naturally to us don’t feel special.

For some people it’s storytelling.
For others it’s systems.
For others it’s seeing patterns in business, relationships, health, or leadership.

But when you’ve spent years developing a skill, solving a problem, or navigating a challenge, what feels obvious to you can be life-changing to someone else.

Your dream customer is usually a version of you from a few years ago.

The person who was stuck where you once were.
The person searching for answers you eventually found.
The person who would have loved someone to show them the path forward.

Entrepreneurship often starts right there.

Find your superpower.
Find the person who needs it.

That’s where businesses (and impact) begin.
