“I’m not ready yet.”

That one sentence has killed more dreams than failure ever will.

You’ll never feel 100% ready.
Not to launch.
Not to post.
Not to sell.
Not to lead.

The belief that you’re “not ready” is exactly why you need to start.
Because clarity doesn’t come from thinking, it comes from doing.

→ Your first product will be messy.
→ Your first funnel will need fixing.
→ Your first pitch might flop.

But you’ll be lightyears ahead of the person still sitting on the sidelines waiting to feel “ready.”

The next level isn’t waiting for perfection.
It’s waiting for movement.

Start before you’re ready.
Grow as you go.
That’s how you build momentum.
