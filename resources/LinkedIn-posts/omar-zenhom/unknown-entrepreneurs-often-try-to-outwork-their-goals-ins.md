Entrepreneurs often try to outwork their goals instead of outgrowing their current skillset.

That works for a while. Then it stalls.

Real progress starts when you treat yourself as the primary asset in your business.

The hours you invest in learning, thinking, and sharpening your value compound faster than any task list ever will. When your capability increases, your opportunities follow.

This episode breaks down a mindset shift that separates people who stay busy from people who actually move forward. It is less about hustle and more about intentional growth.
