Most people think AI means they don’t need a VA anymore.

But here’s the truth I’ve learned after 20 years building businesses:
AI is your power tool.
Your VA is the builder.
You? You’re the architect.

You still need someone who:
– Applies judgment
– Follows through
– Spots mistakes
– Manages chaos
– Owns the outcome

The smartest move today isn’t choosing between AI or humans.

It’s learning how to multiply their strengths.

Especially when your time should be spent on $10K tasks — not formatting emails or chasing invoices.

If you’re building something real, this might be the mindset shift you need.
