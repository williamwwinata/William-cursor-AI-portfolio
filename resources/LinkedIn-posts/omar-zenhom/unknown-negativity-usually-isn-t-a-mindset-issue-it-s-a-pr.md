Negativity usually isn’t a mindset issue. It’s a progress issue.

When nothing is moving, frustration fills the space. That frustration turns into complaining, second guessing, and low energy.

Not because something is wrong with you, but because your brain has no proof that effort is working.

The fastest way out isn’t forcing positivity.
It’s creating visible progress.
Even small wins quiet the noise.

Momentum does the emotional work for you.

Once things start moving, the internal resistance fades and focus comes back.

I break this down in the latest episode on the $100 MBA Show, including how to use progress as a tool instead of trying to “fix” your mindset.

If things have felt heavier than they should lately, this one will hit close to home.
