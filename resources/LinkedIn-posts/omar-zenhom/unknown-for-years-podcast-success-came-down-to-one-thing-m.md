For years, podcast success came down to one thing: make great episodes consistently.

That rule just quietly stopped working.

After thousands of episodes and hundreds of millions of downloads, I’ve seen a major shift in how audiences actually find podcasts today. People are no longer browsing platforms the way they used to. 

Discovery is happening somewhere else entirely, and creators who ignore it are slowly becoming invisible regardless of quality.
This isn’t about trends or chasing algorithms. It’s about understanding distribution. Talent still matters, but reach now determines whether great ideas ever get heard.

If your content feels harder to grow despite improving, this episode explains what changed and how creators should rethink growth going forward.
