One of the most overlooked ways to start a business requires no capital, no credentials, and no big brand.

Events.

Not massive conferences. Not expensive productions.

Simple gatherings that bring the right people together. A dinner. A meetup. A shared experience.

What most people miss is that you’re not selling a venue or food. You’re selling access and connection.

That’s why this model works so well. Cash comes in before costs go out, which removes most of the risk.

If you’ve been stuck waiting for money or permission to start, this episode breaks down why you don’t need either. It’s a practical reminder that business rewards organization and initiative more than resources.
