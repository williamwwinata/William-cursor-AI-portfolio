Most people think higher open rates come from better subject lines.

More emojis.
More urgency.
More tricks.
That’s not it.

People open emails because they trust the sender.

If you only show up to sell…
If your emails feel stiff or generic…
If you disappear for weeks at a time…

You train people not to care.
Stop obsessing over hacks.

Start becoming someone worth opening.
