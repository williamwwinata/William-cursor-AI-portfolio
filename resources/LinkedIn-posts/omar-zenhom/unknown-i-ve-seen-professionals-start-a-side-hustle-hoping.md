I’ve seen professionals start a side hustle hoping it will eventually replace their job.
The problem is most side hustles are designed like hobbies. They produce a few hundred dollars a month but can never realistically replace a full salary.

If the goal is freedom, the business needs to function as an income engine.

A few principles make a big difference:
 • Solve a clear problem that people will pay for
 • Focus on a specific audience that needs that solution
 • Deliver your service in a repeatable way
 • Price the offer so you don’t need to always chase more clients

The surprising part is that the numbers are often smaller than people expect. A few dozen strong clients can replace a full time income.
