One thing Cassandra Sethi said during our conversation in Tokyo stuck with me.

Entrepreneurs will spend $50K on a website…
but forget the most visible part of their brand.

Themselves.

And in the US, saying “I want to look good” can feel taboo. People assume it’s vanity.

Cass sees it differently.
Style is communication.
What you wear signals things before you ever speak.

• Why style is not about trends
