The best people I’ve hired all had one thing in common.
They knew what it felt like to have to prove themselves.

No shortcuts.
No assumptions.
No entitlement.
Just work.

When someone grows up having to earn respect instead of being given it, you see it in how they show up. In how they prepare. In how seriously they take the opportunity.

That kind of hunger is hard to teach.

For entrepreneurs, this is what you’re really looking for. Not just skill. Not just experience.

But someone who treats the role like it matters.
