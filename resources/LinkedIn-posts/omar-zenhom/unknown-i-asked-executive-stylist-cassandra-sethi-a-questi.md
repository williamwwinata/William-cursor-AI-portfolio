I asked executive stylist Cassandra Sethi a question many entrepreneurs are thinking but rarely say out loud.

Has anyone actually made more money after upgrading their style?

She didn’t hesitate: YES.

She’s seen clients get promotions, build stronger professional connections, & even meet partners through improved confidence and presence.

The interesting part is not the clothes.
It’s the signal.

When people feel aligned with how they present themselves, they show up differently.

That confidence changes how others respond to them.

Opportunities often start with something simple like a conversation at a conference, on a train, or after a talk.

Small shifts in how you show up can create those moments.
