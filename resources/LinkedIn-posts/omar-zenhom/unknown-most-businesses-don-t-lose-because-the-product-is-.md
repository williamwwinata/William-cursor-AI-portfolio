Most businesses don’t lose because the product is weak. They lose in the first 30 seconds of explanation.

If people feel confused, they hesitate. If they hesitate, they don’t buy.

A skill almost nobody talks about is the ability to teach your value clearly. When you can explain what you do in simple language, three things happen:
• trust builds faster
• objections shrink
• decisions feel easier

This is not about sounding smarter. It is about removing friction from the buyer’s brain.

The episode walks through how to develop that teaching skill and apply it directly to selling, leadership, and growth
