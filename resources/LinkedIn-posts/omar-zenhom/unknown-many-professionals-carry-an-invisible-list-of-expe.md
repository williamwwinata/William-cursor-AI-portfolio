Many professionals carry an invisible list of expectations.

“I should be healthier. I should be richer. I should be further ahead. I should be more productive.”

Those quiet “shoulds” create constant pressure. No matter how much progress you make, it never feels like enough.

A powerful shift is accepting that you cannot be exceptional at everything. Most areas of life only need to be good enough.

When you reserve your energy for the few things that truly matter, work and life start to feel lighter.
