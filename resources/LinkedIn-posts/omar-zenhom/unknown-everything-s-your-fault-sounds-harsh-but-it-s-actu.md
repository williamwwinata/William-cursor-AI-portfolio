Everything’s your fault.
Sounds harsh, but it’s actually good news.

If your decisions got you here, your decisions can get you somewhere better.

Most people stay stuck because they keep blaming everything else. Circumstances, timing, other people. Feels valid, but nothing changes.

The shift happens when you take ownership. Not blame, ownership. That’s when things start moving.

For entrepreneurs, this is where momentum really begins.
