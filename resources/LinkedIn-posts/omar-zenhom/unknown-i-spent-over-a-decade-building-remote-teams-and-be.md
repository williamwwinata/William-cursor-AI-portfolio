I spent over a decade building remote teams and believed it was the future.

Then I noticed something I couldn’t ignore.
The business wasn’t broken. It was just slower.

Remote work is incredible for lifestyle. It gives flexibility, access to global talent, and lower costs. But when you are trying to scale, speed becomes everything.

Small delays in communication. Slower decisions. Less energy in collaboration. These things compound.
What works for lifestyle doesn’t always work for growth.

If your business feels like it’s lost momentum, it may not be your strategy or your product. It might be your structure.

I share what led me to rethink this and what I am changing in the latest episode of The $100 MBA Show.
