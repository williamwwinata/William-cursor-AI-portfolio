I thought hitting $1M would feel like I made it.
Like that was the goal. The finish line.

It wasn’t.
It actually felt like a new door opened.

Because once you hit it, your thinking changes. You stop asking if it’s possible and start asking how far you can go.

1 million becomes 3 million.
3 becomes 10.

And weirdly, it stops being about money.

It turns into a game. A challenge. How many levels can you reach. How many people can you actually help.

That shift caught me off guard.
For entrepreneurs, this is where things really start.
