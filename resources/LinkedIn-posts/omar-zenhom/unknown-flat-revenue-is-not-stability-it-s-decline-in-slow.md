Flat revenue is not stability.
It’s decline in slow motion.

If your business is flat for two quarters in a row, here’s what is happening behind the scenes:
 • Costs are increasing
 • Competitors are improving
 • Customer expectations are rising
 • Technology is moving forward

Standing still means you’re losing ground.

In the full episode of The $100 MBA Show, I break down how to recognize this early and what practical steps to take to stay in the plus column before flat turns into falling.
