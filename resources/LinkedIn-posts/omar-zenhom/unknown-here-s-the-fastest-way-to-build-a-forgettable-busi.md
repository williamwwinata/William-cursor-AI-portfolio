Here’s the fastest way to build a forgettable business. 

Chase a trend.

The strongest ideas rarely come from watching what everyone else is doing. 

They come from paying attention to what bothers you, what excites you, and the problems you can’t stop thinking about. 

When you finally step away from the noise and start asking better questions, you stop copying the market and start creating something the market hasn’t seen yet.
