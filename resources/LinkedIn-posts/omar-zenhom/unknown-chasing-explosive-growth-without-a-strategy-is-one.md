Chasing explosive growth without a strategy is one of the fastest ways to break your team, your cash flow, and your focus.

The companies that last 100 years rarely grow in wild spikes. They compound small gains over time.
-Small improvements in retention.
-Small improvements in pricing.
-Small improvements in operations.

Applied consistently, those wins stack.

In the full episode of The $100 MBA Show, I explain why steady growth beats dramatic jumps and how to build a business that actually lasts.
