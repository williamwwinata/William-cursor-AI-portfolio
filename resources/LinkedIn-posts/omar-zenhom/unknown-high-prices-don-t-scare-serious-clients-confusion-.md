High prices don’t scare serious clients. Confusion does.

Early in my career, I tried to do everything for everyone. The result:
-Low prices
-High stress
-Endless revisions

Then I did two things:
1. Raised my prices
2. Narrowed my focus

That’s when everything shifted.
Clear positioning led to:
-Better quality clients
-Stronger sales conversations
-Higher margins and healthier cash flow

When someone understands exactly what you do, who it’s for, and why it matters, price becomes secondary.
If you’re constantly defending your pricing, the issue might not be the number. It might be clarity.
