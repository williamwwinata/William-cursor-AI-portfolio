I’ve started over 20 businesses in 25 years.
Only three made me wealthy.

Here’s what changed:
Not intelligence.
Not hustle.
Not luck.

It was choosing the right type of business.

The ones that paid me the most were:
Predictable
Scalable
Less stressful
Almost boring

And that's the part most entrepreneurs miss. We chase excitement instead of structure.
If you're building something right now, ask yourself whether the model itself can realistically create wealth or just income.

I break down the three business types that worked and why the other 17 failed in the full episode on The $100 MBA Show. It might save you years.
