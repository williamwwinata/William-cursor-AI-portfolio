Consistency looks invisible… right before it looks inevitable.

If your posts feel like they’re disappearing into the void, remember what you’re actually up against. 

It’s not just other creators. It’s streaming apps, group chats, and infinite scroll competing for the same attention. 

Standing out rarely happens from one brilliant post. 

It happens when a clear message shows up often enough that people finally start to recognize it.
