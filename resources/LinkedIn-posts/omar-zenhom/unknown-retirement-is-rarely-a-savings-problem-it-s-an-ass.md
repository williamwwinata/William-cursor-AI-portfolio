Retirement is rarely a savings problem. It’s an asset problem.

If your income stops when you stop working, the clock never really turns off. The shift is learning to build something that can eventually be sold, not just worked.

The core idea is simple:
• Stop optimizing for income
• Start optimizing for exit value
• Make decisions that increase sellability
• Treat your business like a future asset, not a job

That mindset changes hiring, pricing, growth, and how you spend your time day to day.
I walk through the full framework and the realistic path behind the 7-year retirement plan in the latest episode of the $100 MBA Show. 

It’s less about fantasy retirement timelines and more about building leverage on purpose.
