Most professionals are using AI for execution.

Writing emails. Summarizing documents. Generating ideas.

The bigger opportunity is using it to improve decision making.

One practical approach is creating a simple decision framework inside AI. You give it context about your business, your goals, and your situation. Then you challenge decisions from different angles.

This doesn’t replace judgment. It sharpens it. You start seeing blind spots faster and thinking more clearly under pressure.
