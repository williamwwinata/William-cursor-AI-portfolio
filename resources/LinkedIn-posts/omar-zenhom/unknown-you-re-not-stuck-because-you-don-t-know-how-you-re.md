You’re not stuck because you don’t know how.

You’re stuck because you keep starting with the wrong question.

Most people begin with “How do I do this?” and immediately feel overwhelmed.

But the real shift happens when you start with why.

When the reason is strong enough, the next step becomes obvious and action gets a lot easier.

The real enemy isn’t confusion. It’s comfort.
Comfort feels safe, but it quietly keeps you exactly where you are.

So if you’ve been sitting on an idea, waiting until everything makes sense…

Don’t wait.

Start with your why, move anyway, and let the how reveal itself along the way.
