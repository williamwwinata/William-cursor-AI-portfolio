One idea from my conversation with executive stylist Cassandra Sethi really stuck with me.

As entrepreneurs grow, their style often stays behind.

You become more experienced.
More capable.
More established.

But your appearance can still reflect an earlier version of yourself.

That disconnect shows up more than people realize.

A few things Cassandra shared that entrepreneurs should think about:
• Your external presentation should evolve as your career evolves
 • People form impressions within seconds of seeing you
 • Style is not about trends. It is about alignment

When your presence matches the level you operate at, people feel it immediately.

That alignment builds credibility before you even speak.
