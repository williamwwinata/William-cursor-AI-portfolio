The fastest way to delay retirement is to treat income like the goal.

Income keeps you busy. Assets buy you time.

If I were starting from zero and wanted out as fast as possible, I would ignore the traditional script and focus on one thing.

Build something that creates value without requiring your daily presence.

That means solving a real problem for a specific group using the skills you already have.

Not someday. Now.

The episode walks through the structure behind that decision and why most people accidentally trap themselves in jobs that look successful but never become sellable.
