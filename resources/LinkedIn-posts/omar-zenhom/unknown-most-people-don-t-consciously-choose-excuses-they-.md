Most people don’t consciously choose excuses.
They just protect their ego in small, comfortable ways.

Blame the timing.
Blame the market.
Blame the algorithm.
Blame anything that removes responsibility.

It feels safer than taking a step that might fail.
But that safety comes with a cost.

Nothing changes.

This isn’t about being harsh.
It’s about being honest enough to move.

Because the moment you stop explaining,
you finally give yourself a chance to make progress.
