Most productivity problems are not about time management.

They are about goal management.

When you try to optimize everything, you dilute your effort and burn out.

The fastest-growing businesses I’ve seen focus on one clear outcome and align everything around it.

Here’s the simple shift that changes everything:
-Choose one goal that drives your business forward
-Cut or delegate anything that does not support it
-Schedule your time around that priority, not around noise

This approach creates momentum fast because your energy is no longer split.
