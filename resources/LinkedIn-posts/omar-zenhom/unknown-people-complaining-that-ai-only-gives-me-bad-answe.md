People complaining that “AI only gives me bad answers,” read on

The problem isn’t bad AI. It’s the way you’re using it.

AI behaves like a highly capable assistant. It can produce strong work, but it has no understanding of your business, your goals, or your standards unless you explain them.

When the input is vague, the output will be average.

What you need to do is treat AI like a new hire. Give it direction, context, and clear expectations. Over time, the quality of output improves significantly.
