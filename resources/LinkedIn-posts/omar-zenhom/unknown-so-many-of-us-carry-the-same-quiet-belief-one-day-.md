So many of us carry the same quiet belief.

“One day everything will finally be handled. The inbox will be empty. The backlog will disappear. Life will feel under control.”

But work regenerates. Tasks return. New problems appear. Waiting to “catch up” is what keeps many people anxious.

The real shift is accepting that life will never be fully cleared. When you stop chasing that illusion, you can focus on what actually matters today.
