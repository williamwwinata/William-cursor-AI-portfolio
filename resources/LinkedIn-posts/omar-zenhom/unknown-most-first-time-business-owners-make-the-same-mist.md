They spend months planning.

Logo. Website. Business plan. Colors. Fonts.

Everything… except actually doing the work.

It’s supposed to look messy.
One job. One customer. One real experience.

That’s where the learning actually happens.

Not in your notes. Not in your ideas. Not in your “perfect plan.”
