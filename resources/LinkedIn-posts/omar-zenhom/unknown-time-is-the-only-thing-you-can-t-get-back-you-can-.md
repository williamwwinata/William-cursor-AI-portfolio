Time is the only thing you can’t get back.

You can make more money. Fix mistakes. Rebuild almost anything. But not a single second of time comes back once it’s gone.

That’s the part most people don’t fully absorb.

Every hour spent on something that doesn’t align with who you want to be… is gone. For good.

The shift happens when you start treating your time like it actually matters. Not casually. Not later. Now.

For entrepreneurs, this is where real clarity comes from.
