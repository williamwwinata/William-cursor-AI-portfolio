Most people think the content game is about consistency.

Post once or twice a week. Stay “active.” Hope for results.

That advice is outdated.

Today, platforms reward volume because volume creates three things most creators never get enough of:
 • Visibility with the algorithm
 • Feedback from real people
 • Data on what actually works

Without enough output, you’re guessing. You’re not learning fast enough to improve.

The goal is not to post forever. The goal is to post enough to find signal, then refine.
