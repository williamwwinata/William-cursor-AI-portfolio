Most conferences are judged by the speaker list.
That’s usually the least important signal.

The conferences that actually move your business forward tend to share three traits:
 • The right people show up, not just big names
 • The content challenges how you think, not just how you feel
 • The environment feels human, warm, and grounded

When those are present, real conversations happen.
Deals form naturally.
Ideas stick longer than the swag.
