The algorithm doesn’t reward effort. It rewards exposure.

Posting “consistently” sounds good, but consistency alone doesn’t create momentum. 

What actually moves the needle is volume and iteration. 

The creators who grow fastest aren’t guessing what will work. 

They publish enough to learn what works, then repurpose the winners so their message shows up everywhere people are already scrolling.
