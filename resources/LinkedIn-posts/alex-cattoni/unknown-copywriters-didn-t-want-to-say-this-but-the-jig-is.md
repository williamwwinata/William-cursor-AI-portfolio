Copywriters. Didn't want to say this but...the jig is up. Copywriting is too easy now with AI. All our clients have to do to write good copy is: 
- Know who their audience is
- Know what their problems are
- Know how to speak to those problems 
- Convey it as efficiently as possible 
- Edit 
- Edit 
- And edit again until it's semi-creative enough 
- Then hire a real copywriter that can actually do all this.
