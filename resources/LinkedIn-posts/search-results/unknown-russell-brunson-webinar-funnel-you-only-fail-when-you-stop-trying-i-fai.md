YOU ONLY FAIL WHEN YOU STOP TRYING



I failed in life too many times if I am brutally honest with myself. I look back with some shame and a lot of annoyance actually because I’ve lost $6-figures in past businesses where in hindsight I should have worked harder. That said, I am being a bit unfair on myself. At the time I must have believed I was giving it my very best shot. And it is unfair to apply the thinking I now have to back then because I have learned so much since those times.



In another post, I have talked about expectations and disappointment and the relationship between them.



If you’ve run any kind of paid ads on social media, you’ll already know that it usually takes dozens of tests before the right combination of image/video, audience, headline and text work – both in the ad, landing page and offer page.



Well, it’s similar with webinars – the first one you ever run is unlikely to earn you any $, same with second and third. You’ll be gathering data and info though for the different steps in your overall funnel.



If you are the kind of person that “gives it a try” then I’d encourage you instead to become the kind of person who “keeps trying until I succeed”. 



Then apply that to your webinar funnel. 



If you were told you needed to run 70 webinars before your webinar worked, would you do it?



You may not have heard of Russell Brunson of ClickFunnels fame, but that’s how many webinars he did to get it right before then automating his webinar funnel.



At one per week that is one year and four months. But he earned $Multiple Millions in doing so!



Russell worked it all out himself from scratch, but you don’t need to. 



I’ve distilled out the best elements from books, courses, other webinars, live in the room public speaking training and just about every resource I could get my hands on in order to create my Winning with Webinars program. You won’t need to do 70 webinars to get there.
