The Real Secret Behind Every 7 and 8-Figure Online Business?

Over the past 20 years in business — growing large online businesses and mentoring 20,000+ students online across 50+ countries — we’ve scaled three major education programs.

Each one looked like a big success from the outside.

But here’s what no one saw:

In every case, the real breakthrough didn’t come from the ads, the funnel, or the algorithm.

It came from one person.

One true believer.

We’ve also been lucky enough to be mentored personally by three legends of this industry: Sam Ovens, Russell Brunson, and Frank Kern.

And the biggest thing we learned from them wasn’t inside a course.
It was in private conversations.

One-on-one. Behind the scenes. The stuff they rarely say out loud.

I remember talking with Sam Ovens, talking about how Consulting became a global force.

He looked at us and said something I’ll never forget:
“It wasn’t what people think. It wasn’t content or ads. It was one JV partner.”

That one partnership — and the trust and transformation that came from it — lit the fire that changed everything.

We had a similar conversation with Russell Brunson.
We asked, “What was the actual turning point behind ClickFunnels?”

He didn’t talk about traffic hacks or the tech stack.

He told us it was one client-turned-partner who believed in his offer and organized a single high-leverage webinar.

That one event converted so well that Russell repeated it every week for the next year. Anyone remember the perfect webinars?

That single webinar became the business.

It eventually helped him turn ClickFunnels into an 9-figure+ empire — built not on ads, but belief.

And then there’s Frank Kern, the godfather of internet marketing.
We asked him: “What made your early success take off?”
He smiled and said:
“Honestly, it was one guy. One person who just believed in what I was doing and promoted me hard.”
No viral campaign. No SEO wizardry.

Now here’s the wild part:

We’ve seen the same exact thing in our own journey over the last 15 years — three different times.

And each time, the movement started with one transformed client who couldn’t keep it to themselves.

They got results.
They told others.
They became the face of the movement.
They were the reason others said,
“If it worked for them, maybe it’ll work for me.”

Your business doesn’t need more noise.
It needs more belief.
And that belief doesn’t come from perfect marketing.

It comes from transforming one person’s life — and empowering them to tell the story.

If you’re a coach, consultant, course creator, or digital founder trying to break through a plateau…

✅ You don’t need 1,000 cold leads.
✅ You need 1 red-hot believer.
✅ Someone who feels changed and can’t stay quiet about it.

Don’t just chase reach. Viral reels.

Build ripple effects — by spotlighting the people who already love your work.
Let your best clients lead the way.

Because behind every big business… there’s always one person who lit the fire.
