Women of LinkedIn who helped me in my Freelancing era: 


This week, I did a little wrap up of all the things Freelancing: how much I made, what work I have done, where I got clients. And today, it deserves something special. So without further ado

1. Our girly whatsapp community by Mia Rowe - Having a supportive community means an absolute world. And even more so, when it comes to a world of Freelancing, just because you work alone doesn't mean you have to be lonely! 

2. Word Tonic by Carolyn McMurray - I have come across their ad for a free webinar and it was so much fun I immediately signed up. In my 2 months there, I have learnt so much. I would recommend it to any copywriter out there, it really has a great sense of community plus you learn something new so often! 

3. Slack group by Louise Rebecca Thomas - The advice given here is golden, people here are very friendly and professional and with them, I always get the sense that I know what I am doing. 

4. How to cold email pitch - by Colleen Welsch - She has an amazing energy and fantastic advice. Definitely give her a go if you are learning in Copywriting. 

5. Videos by Alex Cattoni - I have been watching her videos for months. If I could recommend one youtube channel, it is her. I have learnt so much and she has motivated me to always keep going. 

6. Newsletter by Lizzie Davey - subscribing to her newsletter was an amazing choice for me, she is so transparent and so friendly. 10/10 

7. Whatsapp community by Amy Kohl - This is the last community I am part of and I do not have a bad word to say about it. I have learnt about Social Selling Index on here, I have learnt how to grow on LinkedIn. And I have met many amazing women. 

At the beginning of writing this post, I wanted to focus on sharing the things I have learnt in my 3 months Freelancing. Tools I found most useful. So I started compiling list of people I learnt from. Communities that have been really helpful. 

And I realised that they were all strong women I learnt most from. So cheers to all of them 💕
