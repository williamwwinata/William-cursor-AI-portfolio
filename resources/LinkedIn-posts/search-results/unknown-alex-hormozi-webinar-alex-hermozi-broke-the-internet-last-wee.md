Alex Hermozi broke the internet last weekend.

And if you don't know who this is or what I'm talking about, I wanna know what f*ckin' rock you've been living under.

I didn’t buy - but I’ve been pulling nuggets from other creators and influencers who did. And let me tell you, whether you loved it or hated it, the man put on a masterclass in marketing.

Here’s the truth - marketing is psychological.

Some people clutched their pearls over his tactics - labeling uncomfortable or blurring the line of ethical - citing that he preyed upon the minds and emotions of strugglin entrepreneurs. But nobody was held at gunpoint. Nobody was forced. At the end of the day, a buyer makes a choice based on one thing: perceived value.

Hormozi pulled in over $100M in book sales and bundled upsells.

That’s not luck. That’s the payoff of 5+ years of showing up, giving away insane value, and building a brand people actually trust.

And THAT's the lesson for YOU, Business Owner.

Stop f*ckin' obsessing over “how do I sell more right NOW” and start thinking “how do I become the go-to, trusted resource in my niche so that when I launch, people don’t think twice.”

Love him or hate him, you can’t ignore the results.

And if you’re smart, you’ll stop whining about his style and start asking how you can apply the same principles in your own business.

Because here’s MY hot take - the real flex isn’t making noise for a weekend. It’s building a reputation strong enough that when you do make noise, the internet shows up with wallets open.
