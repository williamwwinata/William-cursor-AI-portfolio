The uncomfortable truth about starting on YouTube ⬇️

My first video was rough. Really rough. But that messy start was the spark that led to over 4 million subscribers across multiple channels.

Here's what 15 years of YouTube has taught me:
💡 Perfectionism kills more dreams than failure ever will
💡 Your first attempt won’t be your best, but it’s your most important
💡 Consistency beats perfection every time

Two principles that changed everything for me: 
✅ Hit record and publish, even when it's messy
✅ You gotta be cringe before they binge

Most entrepreneurs wait for the "perfect moment" to start creating content.
That moment doesn't exist. You create it by starting.
