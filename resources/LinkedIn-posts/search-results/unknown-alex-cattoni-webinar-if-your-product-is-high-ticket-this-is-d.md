If your product is high-ticket, this is definitely for you…


Last week, I attended a webinar on email marketing by Alex Cattoni.

The session was a great one. So many useful tips and strategies.

I’ve taken almost 4 pages of notes during the webinar. 

All thanks to Alex Cattoni. 

Here, I’ll share with you only the 3 key points that I found most useful:


No. 1: Social media is great for building an audience. But the money is in email marketing.

This is especially true if your product is high-ticket.

Because selling high-ticket products requires high trust and which isn’t possible with social media alone.

That’s where email marketing comes into play.

Because it allows you to personalize your message, which is key to building trust and strong relationships with your audience.


No. 2: Storytelling boosts your email engagement.

Stories are powerful because they can keep people engaged for hours (of course, it needs to be a good one).

Powerful stories are how good movies keep their audience engaged for hours.

So, when you start writing story emails…it definitely can keep your audience engaged too.


Bonus Point: Increasing your email engagement boosts email deliverability too.

It’s a WIN/WIN.


No. 3: You don’t need to sell or add links in every email.

Don’t let your customers feel like you email them just to promote or click on something.

Occasionally, send emails with just a story or valuable content without adding any link.

Because it helps strengthen the trust with your audience.

So, when they actually need your services, they will naturally prefer you above all.
