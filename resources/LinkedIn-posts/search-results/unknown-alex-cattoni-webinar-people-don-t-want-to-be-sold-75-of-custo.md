🛑 People don’t want to be sold. 75% of customers say they want a seller-free experience. Give them what they need, let them explore, and trust they’ll come back when they’re ready.

They want to connect. They want context. They want to trust.
Just listened to the always-fantastic Alex Cattoni during the Story Band webinar and whew — it hit hard. A few takeaways I can’t stop thinking about:

-People want to work with people, and what they believe in, more than what you’re "selling." Authenticity wins. Every. Single. Time. 👏

-AI can’t replace the human element. Sure, it’s powerful — but it lacks context. It’s our humanness that adds value and makes experiences memorable.

-We’re in a trust recession. Trust is at an all-time low, and 69% of people distrust brand advertising. That’s wild — and a call to action.

-Build community, not just campaigns.
Community = connection. And connection leads to trust, which leads to action.

So what’s the move?
✅ Be real.
✅ Be human.
✅ Build trust.
✅ Tell better stories.

Thanks to Alex Cattoni and the StoryBrand crew for sparking all the thoughts. I’m ready to bring even more humanity into the work I do.
