Alex Hormozi just made history with his $100M Money Models launch.

It’s being said he sold 9 figures worth of books in a single day.
But here’s the part people miss 

For 3 years, Hormozi didn’t sell anything to the general audience.
 His only offers? $10–$20 books. The real business is Acquisition.com.

So what changed?

He ran a marketing behemoth campaign:
 ✅ ~$6M spent on Meta ads in just 30–40 days
 ✅ The offer? A webinar + a book
 ❌ Not a high-ticket mastermind
 ❌ Not 1:1 coaching

And yet, the perceived value was astronomical.

This wasn’t about creating something new.

Russell Brunson did it. Sabri Suby did it.

But Hormozi and his team executed with such scale and clarity that it felt new.
