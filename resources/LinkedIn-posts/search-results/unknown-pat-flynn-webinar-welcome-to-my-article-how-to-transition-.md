Welcome to my article “How to Transition from CPA Marketing to Affiliate Marketing”. If you have been knee-deep in CPA marketing, chasing clicks and counting conversions like a caffeinated accountant, you might be wondering if there’s a bigger game out there. Spoiler alert: there is! It’s called affiliate marketing, where the stakes are higher, the rewards are sweeter, and the learning curve is slightly more intimidating than trying to assemble IKEA furniture without the manual.
