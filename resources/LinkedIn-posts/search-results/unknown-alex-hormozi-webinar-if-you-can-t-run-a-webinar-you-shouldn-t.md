If you can't run a webinar you shouldn't be selling

Or at least if you can't put one together you shouldn't be

A webinar is NOT

- a product demo
- a long company introduction
- a disguised sales pitch

The best webinars focus on

- insight
- useful ideas
- real problems your customers face

So in theory any salesperson should have the content to put one together

OK there are some delivery techniques involved

But the same core content is what a successful sale is built around

If that stuff isn't in your locker you'll struggle to speak to customers... whether thats 1-to-1 or 1-to-many

If it is maybe there is another format for you

Start 'private' move to 'public'

It's not about breaking Alex Hormozi's world record for attendance

It's about sharing your valuable stuff with people that need it

Would you run a webinar if your 10 best customers/prospects promised to turn up?
