Customers always want you to be everything so they can pay for as little as possible. That's a quick way to go down a rabbit hole and be mediocre at everything you do.

Building software might be easier than ever, but building great software is still incredibly difficult. It's the nuances and details that can't be explained and can only be felt that set you apart from your competition. It's the love you put into your product that people stay for.

From the beginning, we made a decision to be the best webinar automation solution the world has ever seen and nothing else. We would only invest in features that enhance the interactive video delivery and consumption experience.

While that means saying no to a lot of things our customers have asked for, it also means making a concerted effort to integrate with other best-of-class software to give them the end-to-end solution they seek.

Most "feature requests" we get are full blown products that other companies are already nailing. One of those things is AI chat inside eWebinar.

Rather than pretending we can do a better job than others who have already solved this, we integrated Chatbase into our product so our customers can finally have a fully automated webinar without the need to respond manually to every chat message. 🙌 🤖 

This was one of the most seamless integrations we've done. Thanks to the team at Chatbase, especially Sandra Đajic and Yasser Elsaid for the support. 🚀
