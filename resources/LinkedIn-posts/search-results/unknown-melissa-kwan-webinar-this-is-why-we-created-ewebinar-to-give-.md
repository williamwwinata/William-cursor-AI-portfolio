This is why we created eWebinar. To give your customers content when they want it. So you can meet them where they are. Not force them into your schedule.

The average attendance rate across all eWebinar customers is 71%. Anyone who’s done a webinar before knows that’s 2x average.

By enabling on-demand on your eWebinar, you can give access to 100% of your prospect/customer base. Which is impossible to do on a live event.

Like Chris says here: Stop gating content behind a time slot.

When was the last time you didn’t just hit play to watch a show? We’re already there as consumers of lifestyle content. 

Why should business webinars be any different?
