In the last 7 years, I’ve tested every funnel in the book—webinars, upsells, downsells, memberships, subscriptions, you name it.

If a guru named it, I tested it.

Then 2018 happened. I cornered Russell Brunson at a ClickFunnels event and asked for feedback. 

He smiled and said, “Stop building 10 funnels for 10 offers. Build ONE. Scale it. Dominate. Then build the next one.” 

My brain short-circuited. I was juggling agency DFY, coaching DWY, and a DIY course, each with a different funnel. I was “busy.” I wasn’t winning

So I made the shift. I ditched everything else and committed fully to 1 VSL funnel. When we hit a 15% closing rate, everything accelerated. 

We could scale predictably. We could dominate.

Then thanks to one thing,  in 2020, I won my first 2 Comma Club award for generating more than a million dollars with one funnel.

We applied the same principle at ColdIQ.

Instead of spreading ourselves across multiple offers, we zeroed in on one core offer.  Not an Inbound offer..or an Outbound Offer..But a ALL bound, creating a flywheel where Inbound organic marketing, Paid Ads and AI Outbound frameworks work together.

Now we’re targeting $20 million in 2026.

But one main funnel doesn’t mean you abandon everything else.

I still run a webinar funnel (quarterly, live) to connect with the audience and a squeeze funnel (to move social engagement into email).

Here’s the key: VSL remains the engine. Everything else feeds into it.

Until your audience is on your email list, they’re not truly yours. Social followers are fleeting. But committed subscribers? That’s your real asset.

And that’s why I also run quarterly live webinars.

If you had to choose one funnel to dominate, just one, which would it be?

You might be just one funnel away
