This one question from my dad changed the way I approach each and every project I put my name to…

The temptation to cut corners is everywhere. Whether it’s time constraints, pressure from others or just plain laziness, shortcuts are naturally attractive…

The problem with shortcuts, however, is that the results they yield are most always subpar, especially when compared side-by-side with the results of those who put in their very best effort.

It’s ok if your first webinar, first launch or first funnel isn’t perfect – I’m not saying everything you do has to be flawless the first time around. But are you continually trying to improve with each iteration? Are you stopping at “just ok”, or are you working until you can step back and honestly say you’re proud of what you made? #entrepreneur #entrepreneurship #russellbrunson #personaldevelopment
