🚀 Unlocking Momentum in Business 🚀

The theme of Adcon 2025 training is all about building Momentum. Jon Penberthy shared  Why so many people struggle to maintain momentum in business?

🔍 Key Reasons 
	1.	Lack of Confidence
	2.	Shiny Object Syndrome 🪩
	3.	Missed Opportunity
      4.   Getting in your own head 

But here’s the secret to overcoming these challenges: Control. Take charge of your path and watch momentum follow.

✨ Building a Solid Foundation: WORK ON YOU 
	1.	Offer: Create an irresistible offer that clients can’t say no to. most coaches and consultants make the mistake of creating to many offers . You only need one offer to get you to 50k per month 
	2.	Brand: Build a strong brand with clear core values.
	3.	Operations: Keep track of your metrics and ensure smooth operations with your team.

💡 Traffic vs. Conversion: You don’t have a traffic problem … you have a conversion problem, you can just buy as many traffic as you want
It’s not just about driving traffic; it’s about converting that traffic. Focus on turning visitors into loyal customers.

🚀 Traffic Strategies 🚀
	1.	Buy It: Use paid ads like Facebook and Instagram ads to quickly drive traffic.
	2.	Build It: Create valuable content on platforms like YouTube and LinkedIn to attract and engage your audience.
	3.	Borrow It: Collaborate with others through podcasts or presentations to tap into their audience and drive traffic your way.

And your marketing strategy? That’s your engine.
Start with an opt-in page to capture leads. Then, have a conversion strategy in place..whether it’s through webinars, challenges, or live workshops…to turn those leads into customers/ clients …..This is exactly what I help my clients build..complete sales funnels that capture and convert, so they can scale their business effectively.


Depending on your offer you can offer one to one or one to many 

💡 Follow-Up: Remember, a lead often needs a second chance. Use email sequences to nurture and convert them.

Keep these strategies in mind, and you’ll effectively boost your traffic and conversions! 🌟

🔧 PSP Method: Problem - solution- problem 
Identify your client’s Problem, offer a Solution, and address their evolving needs to keep them engaged and get them better results as they ascend in avatar level

🎁 Wrap It Up:
Present your offer In new creative ways—whether as challenges (2 days challenge, 30 days Challenge….) , eBooks , virtual event , webinar …Make them irresistible!

Which part of this resonated with you the most? And What’s one strateg you will apply to build momentum in your business?
