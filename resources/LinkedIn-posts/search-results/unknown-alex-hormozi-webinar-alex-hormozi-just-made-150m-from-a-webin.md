Alex Hormozi just made $150M+ from a webinar.

Here’s the ONLY reason why it worked:

The brand/reputation he’s built.

Alex has been giving away insane amounts of value for the past 3 years straight. 

His motto was literally, “I have nothing to sell you.”

And I’ve become a big fan. 

But, this was always part of a bigger move. 

He knows that the more trust you build, the more money you can make.

So, he maximized his trust.

Crazy good YouTube videos, insanely valuable books, and super intriguing podcast appearances. 

This has resulted in millions of followers.

But more importantly, millions of people that trust him.

And this is ultimately why he could pull this off.

Sure, he spent a ton of ads.

Yes, he planned and executed very well. 

But getting 25k people to donate 200 of his books? 

That’s NEXT level. 

Love him or hate him, he’s crushing it. 

Here’s the key takeaway: 

If you want to build wealth, build trust. 

Be willing to give before asking. 

The more you give first, the more you can ask later.
