Had the pleasure of chatting with Jason Fladlien on Episode 226 of #TheBuilders and this quote from him hit hard.

Such a good reminder. We spend so much time perfecting what we think is valuable… but if it’s not resonating with the people we’re trying to serve, it falls flat. That shift—from our lens to theirs—can change everything.

Jason brought so much insight in this episode. We talked webinars, AI, choosing the right business games to play…and this theme of focusing on what really matters to your audience came up more than once.

Give it a listen—Jason’s one of the sharpest minds I’ve had on the show.
