I just finished Alex Hormozi’s new book.

For many, it will feel like a revelation.
For me, it was more like stepping into a time machine, revisiting the fundamentals that shaped an entire generation of growth experts.

And that’s Hormozi’s true gift: taking timeless marketing principles, reshaping them in a fresh way, and making them accessible and powerful for today’s entrepreneurs.

If you weren’t an entrepreneur around between 2010 and 2015, it makes sense this feels revolutionary.

But if you were there, you’ll remember…

	•	Russell Brunson and the Value Ladder (I had the privilege to meet him in person and join his Inner Circle, where I learned the “value ladder” & how to increase lifetime value step by step).
	•	Stu McLaren, who I saw live at Funnel Hacking Live, he transformed memberships into one of the most strategic and scalable models out there.
	•	Frank Kern and his irresistible offers.
	•	Rich Schefren, with systems as the ultimate competitive edge.
	•	Ryan Deiss and The Invisible Selling Machine, turning email into a silent but unstoppable machine.
	•	Mike Filsaime & Andy Jenkins, pioneers who made webinars into mass-selling weapons.
	•	Ryan Levesque, who brought quiz funnels and market analysis to another level.
	•	And even a century back, Claude Hopkins, already teaching us to test, measure, and improve.

👉 The fundamentals never change. Only the voice that shares them does.
