He broke all the rules. Created his own category. Scaled to $300M.

Here’s how Russell Brunson made ClickFunnels the go-to builder for sales funnels:


BACKGROUND

While most SaaS founders focus on features, Russell built something different: a movement.

He didn’t just want users he wanted believers.

The result?

$300M in revenue
A cult-like community
A new job title: Funnel Builder

Here’s how he did it:

1. PAID MEDIA MASTERY

Russell didn’t wait for people to find ClickFunnels.
He flooded the market with ads. Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn.

And not just generic promotions. He built a paid media engine that educated while it sold:

- Webinars that unpacked his funnel strategies
- Case studies that showed real results
- 5-Day Challenges that pulled people into his ecosystem

While competitors relied on word of mouth, Russell controlled the narrative.

2. BUILDING A MOVEMENT

While most SaaS companies sell features, Russell built identity.
ClickFunnels wasn’t just software it was a community.

Here’s how:

- One Funnel Away Challenge:
A 30-day bootcamp that taught entrepreneurs how to build high-converting funnels. And the only tool they needed? 
ClickFunnels.

- Comma Club Awards:
Hit $1M in sales with ClickFunnels? You got a plaque. 
$10M? A bigger one. It turned customers into brand ambassadors.

Job Creation: 
He made Funnel Builder a real job title. 
Marketers branded themselves as experts in his ecosystem.
ClickFunnels users would put out hiring posts for Funnel Builders.

Russell didn’t just want you to use ClickFunnels he wanted you to identify with it.

3. CATEGORY CREATION

Unbounce, Leadpages, Instapage sold landing pages.
ClickFunnels sold sales funnels.

He redefined the product from “a page builder” to “an end-to-end sales journey.”
While others fought for market share, he claimed an entire category.

4. BIG BETS & BOLD MOVES

Russell didn’t follow the typical SaaS playbook.

No chasing VCs.
No feature bloat.
No waiting for organic growth.

He bet big on:

Paid media to create demand
Challenges to build community
Awards to drive loyalty

The result?

ClickFunnels became the default choice for online sales funnels.

$300M in revenue. Zero outside funding.
