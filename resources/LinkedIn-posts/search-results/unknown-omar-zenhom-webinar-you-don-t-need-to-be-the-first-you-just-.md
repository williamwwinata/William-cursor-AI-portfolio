You don’t need to be the first.
You just need to be the one who stays focused.

When I launched WebinarNinja, there were already plenty of webinar tools on the market. I didn’t flinch. I didn’t back off. I saw it as validation.

Here’s what I did instead:
• I focused on solving the problem better
• I built it for my people, not everyone
• I stayed consistent—and ignored the noise

That focus built us a business valuable enough to be acquired.
Not because we were early. Because we didn’t get distracted.
