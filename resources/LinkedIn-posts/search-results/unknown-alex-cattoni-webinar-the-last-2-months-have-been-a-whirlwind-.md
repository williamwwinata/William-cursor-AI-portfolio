The last 2 months have been a whirlwind, between building YOUmanize™, traveling for several events with Joshua B. Lee and expanding my thought capacity in major ways. I’ve been sitting with a few moments that really stayed with me, and one of those happened at Archangel Nexus in Los Angeles. 

What is the quest you're on? 💜 

That’s what Giovanni Marsico asked us on day 1. Not our next launch or quarterly goal, but our deeper quest that sits underneath all of it. When I sat with that question, I realized my answer was simpler and bigger than I expected.  

My quest is to create a movement that sparks trust and confidence so people own their voice, authentic story, and lived experiences. Through my own journey as a Jewish woman, a stepmom, a mom, a wife, a daughter, an employee, a founder, a speaker, and a lecturer, I have seen how powerful it becomes when people stop hiding the parts of themselves that actually make them credible. 

When people lead humanity, connection, and integrity, everything changes. And right now, the path I'm building to bring that mission to life is YOUmanize™ Your Brand™.
