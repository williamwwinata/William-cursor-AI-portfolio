My brain is buzzing and I need time to digest it all.

3 days at #BusinessMania hosted by Adam Stott and Big Business Entrepreneurs

What a phenomenal 3 days - inspiring coaches, guest speakers and business owners, and a 1st class support and event team.

Kicked off with Steve J. & Andrea Marsh Dipl.Coach full of energy. 

James Francis shared many insights from a truly inspirational multi career on mindset and mastery. 

We heard Damien Elston share his philosophy and converting a room of ~500 to 212ers! 

Anna Mosley then bossed how to be the CEO of your life.

Some inspiring business owners had on stage coaching/ interviews from Adam Stott and Ross Temple. 

Jon Penberthy spent time breaking down the scale of cold to warm marketing leads and the importance of webinars. 

Jason Graystone blew us all away with the power of YouTube as a core marketing strategy.

Apprentice winners Sian Gabbidon and Lee McQueen were interviewed on stage and had us all hooked on every word.  

4 x TedX speaker David JP Phillips had us rapt in his talk about controlling dopamine, oxytocin, endorphins and testosterone to impact our mental strength and performance. Phenomenal.

Dilan Abeya showed the power of AI as a tool in business and wowed as he demo'd AI Adam to the the crowd.

Imran Ahmed delivered a powerful session on self belief and driving our inner narrative to get amazing results.

Lastly WOW - Joe Wicks - absolutely blown away by his humility and desire to improve lives. 

And ... I got to interview Joe Wicks live. What a genuinely incredible man. More to follow.

These are just some of the highlights ... what an absolutely exhausting, thrilling, inspirational, kick in the a**e couple of days 🤩
