Most people cringe at their first iterations of their branding, socials, website or even their business. 

Weirdly, I don't anymore. Yes I look back and laugh because I didn't know any better. I did the best I could do with the knowledge I had so it would be slightly unfair to judge myself on that. 

Now? I'm just proud of myself. Because I didn't know anything and I did it anyway. That takes gumption, resourcefulness and self-belief. 

We didn't come out of the womb knowing anything about running a business so why are we so hard on ourselves for at least trying? 

How can I not be proud of that? I've taken A LOT of messy action and I'm fully aware that I didn't even know what I didn't know. And I still don't know so much. 

But isn't that a good thing? 

Life would be hella boring if we knew it all or we were perfect from the get go.
There would be no place to go, no personal development, no curiosity. And certainly no improvements. 

And 30k people wouldn't be showing up to watch Alex Hormozi on a webinar for hours on a Saturday if we all knew it all anyway? 

I dare you to write down what you are proud of. You might even change your perception of your business journey.
