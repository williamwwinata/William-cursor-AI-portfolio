Readiness is a myth.
Nobody feels ready.
Not at the beginning. Not at the next level. Not ever.

You don’t get ready by waiting.
You get ready by moving.

Want to know how I learned to launch webinars?
I launched bad ones.
Want to know how I built funnels that convert?
I built a LOT that didn’t.

Every win I’ve had came AFTER I started.
Never before.

Action creates confidence.
Motion builds momentum.

So if you’re sitting there waiting to “feel ready” before you launch, post, publish, or pitch…

Just know this:

You’re not supposed to feel ready.
You’re supposed to move anyway.
