Why you should hire character over skill

Jason Fladlien, Alex hormozi's  launch and webinar consultant was saying, 

He has a copywriter who's underdeveloped in a copy but has a great character. 

The thing is

teaching copy is very easy 

you can learn it in no time. 

But 

Teaching characters is close to impossible. 

You can't teach someone to be on time. 

Teaching persistence is very difficult. 

A person with great skill but bad personality will be very hard to deal with. 

Before hiring, don't just see skills. 

See the character of that person.
