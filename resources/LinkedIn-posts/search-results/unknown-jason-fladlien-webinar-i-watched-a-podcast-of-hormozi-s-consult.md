I watched a podcast of Hormozi's consultant. 
Understanding this "1-liner" from him is crucial for any copywriter.

The line:

"How can we change the way people relate to the emotions they feel around the actions they know they need to take?"

Now, Jason Fladlien (Hormozi's consultant) has consulted launch webinars for some of the biggest names including Alex Hormozi, Iman Gadzhi, Dean Graziosi and Tony Robbins.

According to him, If you understand this one sentence, you'll get rich as a copywriter. 


Let me breakdown this sentence for you:


1. The Action

What is the action a person knows they need to take?
(sending a proposal, launching a product, having a tough conversation.)


2. The Emotion

What emotions are currently tied to that action?
(fear, resistance, anxiety, shame, excitement, etc.)


3. The Relationship

How does the person interpret or react to those emotions?

Do they see the emotions as signals to stop, or as part of the process?


4. The Shift

How can we help them reinterpret those emotions in a way that supports the action?

What practices, reframes, or tools can transform the emotional response?



Example:

Action: Join a mastermind group

Emotion: Imposter syndrome 

Current Relationship: "I'm not ready yet"

The Shift: 

"Feel like you don't belong here?
Perfect. 
That means you're in a room where you can grow. 
The best masterminds are full of people who felt exactly like you."


See? The emotion didn't disappear. The story around it did. 

That's copywriting at its finest.
