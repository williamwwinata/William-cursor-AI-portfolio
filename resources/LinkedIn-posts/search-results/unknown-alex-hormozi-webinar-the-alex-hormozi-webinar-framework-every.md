The Alex Hormozi webinar framework everyone copies but nobody understands…

Most creators spend weeks obsessing over the "Stack," the countdown timers, and the high-energy delivery. 

They think the magic is in the presentation.

They’re wrong.

Hormozi uses Russell Brunson’s "Perfect Webinar" structure, sure. 

But the real power (the reason he can fill a stadium and move millions in a single afternoon) isn't the slides or his energy.

It’s the POSITIONING.

Most gurus position themselves as the "Hero." 

Hormozi positions the Mechanism.

He doesn't say, "Look at how smart I am." 

He says, "Look at how effective 'Money Models' are."

By shifting the spotlight from himself to his methodology, he creates something far more scalable than a personal brand: 

He creates a New Opportunity.

How to apply the "Hormozi Pivot" to your brand:

If you want to move from "commodity" to "category of one," you need to stop selling your time and start selling your system. 

Here is the breakdown:

Step 1: Name the Mechanism. 

Don't just offer "marketing services." 

Offer the [Your Name] Growth Engine. 

Give it an identity that exists outside of you.

Step 2: Vilify the Old Way. 

Hormozi doesn't just promote his way; he explains why the "standard" way (low-ticket, high-churn) is a death trap.

Step 3: Make the Outcome Inevitable. 

If the system is the hero, then anyone who uses the system can win. 

It removes "you" as the bottleneck for your client's success.

The Lesson:

People will eventually get tired of a personality. 

They never get tired of a mechanism that solves their biggest problem.

So…

Stop trying to be the most interesting person in the room, and…

Start building the most effective "Money Model" in your niche.

Are you positioning yourself or your methodology? Which do you think is more valuable in the long run?
