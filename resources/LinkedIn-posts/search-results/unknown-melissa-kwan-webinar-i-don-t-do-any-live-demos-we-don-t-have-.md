I don't do any live demos, we don't have a salesperson. Yet, 3892 prospects saw our demo in 2025 with a trial conversion of 10.7%. 🤯

97.6% of people opted to watch our demo immediately (on-demand or replay), rather than scheduling a future session.

That's an average of 324 demos every month. How many people would you need on your team to deliver that? For most companies, this would not be humanly possible, nor would this many prospects request a demo (and show up) if they had to speak to a salesperson.

Last year, I saved 2065 hours from NOT doing demos live. That's 2065 hours I got to put towards myself, friends, family, or doing nothing at all. 🔥

In my first two startups, doing demos, training, and onboarding was my life. My entire schedule was designed around my customers' schedule; even when we were profitable, I was never truly free. I hated that life.

I founded eWebinar to solve the most painstaking problem I lived with: doing the same webinar over and over again. 🥵 I promised myself that I would never do another demo live because of how much it infringed on my lifestyle.

eWebinar turns any video into an interactive webinar (with async chat) that anyone, anywhere in the world can join any time and get the same experience. We use our own product to deliver our demo (a very meta experience).

I rarely talk about my own company on here, but this is too awesome not to share.

Perhaps, like the old me, you're also doing the same demo/webinar over and over again and wish you could be doing literally anything else. We have the solution for you - this could change your life as it did mine.

If you're asking yourself...

Are pre-recorded webinars as good as live? 
👉 They're even better than live because your customers can actually join them. Our attendance rate is 80.6%. Live webinars have insanely low attendance rates for a reason. That is the norm, but that doesn't have to be your reality.

Are they as engaging as live?
👉 The premise of our software is to make them engaging throughout with interactions (polls, hotspots, tips, quizzes, etc). Let's face it, how many live webinars have you actually enjoyed?

👉 Are people going to like pre-recorded webinars?
Yes. When was the last time you caught a show on network tv vs on-demand on Netflix? The stats speak for themselves.

Nobody wants to wait to consume content they want RIGHT NOW anymore. Neither do you.
