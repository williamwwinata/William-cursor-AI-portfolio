Ever find yourself drawn in by some compelling marketing. . .

. . .just to almost immediately get turned right around & tuned right out?

You’re not alone & it seems inbox fatigue isn’t the problem. . .
( but predictable, templated "funnels" absolutely might be 🫣) 

As Scott Oldford said earlier this evening: 

"Most entrepreneurs:
 Pick a random funnel type.
 Copy someone else’s webinar.
 Write emails they don’t believe in.
 Glue together five tech tools.
 Launch once.
 Hope it works.
 Quit when it doesn’t.
 That’s not strategy. That’s emotional roulette."

& Honestly. . .EXACTLY!🚨

I’ve been noticing a lot of SUCCESSFUL, well-meaning creators out there still flood inboxes with the same templates, the same webinars, the same emails. Familiar, predictable, and easy to skim past before you even get to the first line.

As per Scott. . .

The future of marketing isn’t more templates. . . 

🧠 It’s contextual intelligence 🤓 
(knowing what, when, and how to act
in a way that actually moves the needle)

I've always fully supported innovation especially towards efficient productivity. . .but "the answer" CAN'T BE plopping everything into ChatGPT or throwing a new dress on the same tired marketing & offering it up to the funnel gods.

Shoutout to creators who really get it . . . like Laura Belgray, Mimi Z., Luke Matthews, Alex Cattoni, Eddie Shleyner, Jasmin Alić, Tarzan Kay Kalryzian . . . all who champion real copywriting with personality, humanity, and real human voice. . .marketing that actually works in an age where it seems everyone is leaving their "human touch" on the cutting room floor.

I'm always excited to discover fresh voices and inspiring creators. . .so if someone amazing came to mind, totally tag them or drop a shoutout in the comments (I’d love to check them out)! 💫
