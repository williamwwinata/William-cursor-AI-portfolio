Alex Hormozi taught me 

how to think about webinars and by extension, how to think about any free offer designed to pull someone toward a paid one.

The framework is simple: break your service into steps. 

If you have ten steps, give away steps one and two for free. 

Teach them generously. Then the person is standing at step three, they want to keep going, and you have a buyer.

I used this directly. I ran a free webinar on prompt engineering for sales professionals. 

I taught the first principles hard. Over fifty people paid to join my AI sales class afterwards.

That is the power of a well-structured free offer. It’s not a trick. It’s a demonstration.

 Show people you can help them, and they will pay you to continue.
