Too many funnels make this horrible mistake.

And cut their chances of success by 50% from the jump.

After 8 years of forging funnels for the likes of Alex Hormozi, AI Report, Sermo, and more… 

I’m still amazed at this HUGE missed opportunity.

You send traffic to the offer page - be it a webinar, lead magnet, product page, etc.

And as soon as the lead converts, they’re relegated to the email list.

Maybe they engage again, maybe not.

Instead, you MUST extend the customer journey.

Here’s what to do.

✅ Add an order bump. A simple, bolt-on offer that promises to deliver the funnel's desired outcome in less time or with less effort.

✅ Add 1 or 2 special one-time-only offers. A more advanced version of the original offer that adds extra step by step support through 1 on 1 or small group implementation. They only get this offer if they take action that day.

✅ Add a next steps page. Tell leads exactly what to expect and what to do next (best in a video), including access to your community.

Leads that fall into your funnel want different things. Some want it DFY, some want to put in the effort, and some need help with implementation. 

You’ve already paid for the traffic, and you do your customers a disservice by not seeing to each level of need.

Extend your funnel to maximize traffic revenue, but more importantly, guarantee that each lead gets offered the service level that they desire.

How many steps does your funnel have? 👑
