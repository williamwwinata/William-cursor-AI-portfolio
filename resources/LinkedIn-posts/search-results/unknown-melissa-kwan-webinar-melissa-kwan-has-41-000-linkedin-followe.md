That's a 97% attention leak.

She posts 3x a week. Gets real engagement — founders sharing real reactions. Built a following without a marketing team, paid ads, or growth hacks.

Here's the gap nobody's talking about.

Her LinkedIn audience follows her for bootstrapping lessons, anti-VC takes, and founder mental health. eWebinar sells to ops managers and CS teams evaluating webinar automation.

Two completely different audiences.

The result:

41,000 LinkedIn followers — only 1,000 on her email list

No outbound. No cold email. No LinkedIn outreach to buyers.

Substack is about founder life — not a nurture engine for webinar buyers

She's built an incredible attention asset. It's just not connected to the revenue engine.

Goal: 500 new subscribers/month from organic traffic.

Every commenter checked against ICP. Matches get a connection request, 3-message sequence, newsletter opt-in. Dead engagement becomes pipeline.
