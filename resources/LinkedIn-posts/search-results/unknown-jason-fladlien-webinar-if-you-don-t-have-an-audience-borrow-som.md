If you don't have an audience, borrow someone else's.

You'll probably get richer this way.

Look, if you don't have an audience right now... or maybe you DO have one, but you've spent months (or even years) building it... this is for you.

Because I'm going to kill one of the biggest myths in the online space.

The myth that you need to build an audience to make money.

You don't.

You can make MILLIONS of dollars without one.

I know because I did it both ways.

I started by building my own audience.

The hard way.

Posting. Emailing. Grinding out content. Showing up consistently.

And yeah, it worked.

But it was slow as hell.

Then I shifted.

I started leveraging other people's audiences as an affiliate.

That's when things blew up.

Suddenly I wasn't waiting for MY list to grow... I was tapping into lists that already had tens of thousands of engaged buyers.

I wasn't hoping the algorithm would bless me... I was getting in front of people who were READY to buy.

The money came faster than it ever did building my own thing.

Then I went up another level.

I started partnering with people who had MASSIVE audiences.

Instead of just promoting their stuff as an affiliate... we were building offers together, splitting revenue, scaling faster than I ever could alone.

And that's when it hit me:

Building my own audience was holding me back.

Because once you know how to CONVERT an audience... there's a million audiences you can tap into.

You don't need to spend years building your own.

You just need to know how to sell.

Building an audience is NOT a prerequisite for making money.

It's a BYPRODUCT of making money.

Think about it.

When you borrow someone else's audience and actually deliver results... guess what happens?

Some of those people follow YOU.

You get testimonials. You get proof. You get momentum.

And THAT'S what builds an audience.

Not posting motivational quotes for six months hoping someone notices.

Not "showing up consistently" with no money coming in.

Results build audiences. 

Not the other way around.

I see people spending 12 months "building their brand" before they ever try to sell anything.

That's backwards.

You know what's faster?

Find someone with an audience.

Offer to do a webinar together.

Split the revenue.

Make $10K in 90 minutes.

Now you have proof. Testimonials. Confidence.

And suddenly, building YOUR audience becomes infinitely easier.

Because you're not starting from zero..

Most people use "building an audience" as an excuse to delay the scary part.

Selling.

Because if you don't have an audience, you can't fail at selling, right?

Wrong.

You're just failing slower.

I spent YEARS making millions before most people knew my name.

And when people finally started paying attention to me as a "personal brand"... it wasn't because I posted my way there.

It was because I had results.

The audience came AFTER that.
