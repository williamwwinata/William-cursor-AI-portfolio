Which business are you in?

If you’re like 99.987% of businesses out there… you’re in the BELIEFS business.

I was watching the interview with Matthew Volkwyn and Jason Fladlien (he’s made millions with webinars, worth watching btw).

Here’s the link: https://lnkd.in/gfAyghHa

One thing Jason said slapped me:

“We’re SHIFTING the beliefs the audience NEEDS shifted in order to be sold on the NEXT step.”

Instantly, I pictured a Rube Goldberg machine…

Each ramp, magnet, or domino nudging the ball forward to keep it rolling.

Same thing goes with ads, emails, webinars… any copy

The big idea, angles, frames…

Each should shift the next belief in line to sell the next step…

Just long enough to keep buyer intent rolling UNTIL they buy.

That’s the real game: 

Moving people belief by belief until the decision’s made.

And every piece of copy?

It’s just another element to shift that belief.

In my opinion, that’s the biggest difference between a content writer VS copywriter.

It’s not just about words, it’s about BELIEFS.

Interview’s definitely worth a watch.
