Jon Penberthy - the talk I didn't know I needed!

He launched his first business - a course on YouTube - aged 19, and went on to be the founder of AdClients and AdCon, London's largest marketing conference. 

What a masterclass in speaking about how to turn challenges into opportunities. He spoke of persistence, passion and innovation underpinning everything he does.

The stuff that really resonated with me:

1. 💡"If I want a result, that result means more to me that the feeling of nerves, discomfort and worry."

2. 💡"You don't remember what they say, but you do remember how they make you feel."

3. 💡"Traffic on YouTube is so valuable; they are actively looking for something."

4. 💡"Webinars; the gold standard in my business."

It was an absolute privilege to listen to him speak ... ☺️
