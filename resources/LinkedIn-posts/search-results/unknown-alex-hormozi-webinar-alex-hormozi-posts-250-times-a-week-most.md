Alex Hormozi posts 250+ times a week.
Most B2B companies struggle to promote one webinar per month.
The difference?

He isn't working harder. He is repurposing smarter.

You spend thousands of dollars and hours of expert time creating one "Big Asset" a webinar, a detailed report, or a podcast.

It gets posted once. Then it dies in your archives.
That is a waste of resources.

The top 1% of B2B brands don’t let content die. They build an ecosystem around it.

Here is the formula we implement for our clients to turn 1 asset into 4 weeks of content:

1. The Source:
One hour-long webinar or podcast (The "Heavy Lift").

2. The Transformation (Long-to-Long):
• We turn the audio into a deep-dive SEO Blog Post (ranking for key terms).
• We extract the data into a downloadable Whitepaper.

3. The Distribution (Long-to-Short):
• 5 LinkedIn text posts + graphics (for the C-suite).
• 15 Vertical Videos (Shorts/Reels) for brand awareness.
• 10 Tweets/X threads.

Same core idea. Different containers. 6 Platforms.

We combine AI speed with human strategy to make this happen without adding a single hour to your team's workload.

Stop creating from scratch every time.

Start treating your knowledge like an asset that compounds.

That is how you dominate your niche.
