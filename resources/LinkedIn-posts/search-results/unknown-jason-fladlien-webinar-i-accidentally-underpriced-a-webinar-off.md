I accidentally underpriced a webinar offer.

Instead of fixing it quietly, I said: “If you buy before this ends, I’ll honor it.”

People rushed in.

For 2 seconds I thought “Why not fake this every time?”

Then came the breakthrough:

Don’t fake urgency.
Stack value so high the price feels like theft.
Ethically.
