Day 2 at SBC25 another great start to the day networking and catching up with people.

9:15 am Paul Scanlon started the day by discussing all things leadership and his main philosophy Influence not authority. 

10:15 am Jon Penberthy gave us a 90 minute quick run through of his Momentum Framework which is all about digital marketing as well as his belief that webinars are the best way to help convert leads.

11:20 am Gary Rhodes discussed the importance of health including screening blood test's and basically looking after yourself as you are the number one commodity in your business.

1:05 pm Paul Rawson discussed Express Mortgages and his business relationship with Paul McFadden as well as there approach to clients first within there business.

1:30 pm Paul McFadden returned to go through his new training programs and how Paul Pmw can help business owners build and scale there business as part of this my sister Michelle De Almeida Almeida was used as a case study of what Paul and his team can help you achieve incredibly proud of my sister a true inspiration to me.

3:20 pm Sarah Willingham told her life story and then took the time to answer questions from both Paul and the audience covering all things business.

5:00 pm As part of our Inner Circle Ticket we got to spend time with the amazing Dan Peña - The Trillion Dollar Man the audience got to ask questions and tap into this amazing persons knowledge. It was a pleasure to listen to what this man had to say. An individual who has lead an incredible life and truly has no fear.

7.00 pm We attended an evening dinner at the exchange in Glasgow city center pre-dinner drinks, three course meal with speakers and then a dance we got back to the hotel around 11 pm having had another great day.
