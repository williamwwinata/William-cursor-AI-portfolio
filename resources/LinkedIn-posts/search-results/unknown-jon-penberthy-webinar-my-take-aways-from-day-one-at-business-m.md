My take aways from day one at Business Mastery Live in Leeds…

Matt Hall  on email marketing: send at least once a week, good subject lines, write like you speak, use storytelling 

Regina Martin  on websites: use H1 once per page, one page per service, no walls of text, captions on all videos

Jon Penberthy  Traffic from paid ads, build traffic on my platforms, borrow audiences. Use the Dream100 list! Webinars for marketing and the power of 2 sales calls 

AI panel - so much I can’t even write it down here! 

Daniel Priestley  my story and ideal client, it’s all about personal brand! How to be a key person of influence 

Amy Atkinson  on messaging and sales from social media - what are your clients worried about? What’s their struggle? Get emotions involved! 

Lindsay McGlone how to use Instagram properly! Lots of gold on collabs, positioning, audience nurturing and sales 

As well as LOTS of networking opportunities, great catch up with all of my business mates! 

So many ideas hatching in my head!
