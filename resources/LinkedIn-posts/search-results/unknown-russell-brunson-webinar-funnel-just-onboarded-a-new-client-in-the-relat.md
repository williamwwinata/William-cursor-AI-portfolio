Just onboarded a new client in the relationship niche.

He told me he knows to sell from events, 
he has done hundreds in the past.

I sensed a little ego and said,

“Great! Then we must not have much to work on…”
 
But then I asked,

“Why haven’t you done any since 2023?”

He said,

“Webinars don’t work anymore. 
People don’t buy like they used to.”

I almost burst out laughing, tears and all.

Russell Brunson spent an entire year screaming about selling one-to-many events.

Not to mention all the other top sellers are still crushing it with live events.

And this guy says people don’t buy from webinars anymore?

Ha… what an excuse.

“Yes, buying behaviors have changed” …he continued.

True, people don’t read long funnels or testimonials anymore, they scroll straight to the price.

But here’s the huge leverage:
Stick them to the screen with a powerful story.
 
Open with a hook that grabs attention, like the story 
model in the photo below.

Then bridge the gap between where they are now… and the dopamine-driven future they want.

live events, write one and send me your copy.
