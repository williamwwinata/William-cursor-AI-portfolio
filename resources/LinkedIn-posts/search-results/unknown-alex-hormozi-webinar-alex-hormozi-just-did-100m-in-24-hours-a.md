Alex Hormozi just did $100M+ in 24 hours. And broke the world record 🤯

Everyone is saying it was only because he has a huge audience.

It wasn’t.

The engine behind it was the biggest affiliate cold email funnel ever built.

The affiliates send $3M+ emails everyday. 

If you wanna copy his strategy to your business here’s how you can:

1. Build an affiliate partner system:

- Offer 20% rev share on every closed deal (so if you close a $5K deal, they make $1K).

- Target 30 cold email partners who already have good deliverability setups.

- Give them pre-approved scripts, assets, and a tracking system so they can just plug in and send.

- Keep payouts fast and transparent affiliates stick when trust is high.

2. Build a Slack / Skool  community:

Start with free guides + short courses. 
Keep people close and nurtured.

3. Run weekly webinars:

Bring in respected industry voices (like Adam Robinson Robinson does). 

Makes your brand look 10x bigger.

4. Turn customers into marketers:

Offer small discounts if they share their experience on LinkedIn. 

Word of mouth travels fast.

5. Test new offers constantly:

Fresh angles = fresh demand.

6. Content machine:

Push your team to publish 5–10 posts a week. 

If they won’t, buy warmed LinkedIn accounts

(we’ve done this for 5+ YC companies, works insanely well).

7. Go multichannel:

Don’t stop at LinkedIn. 

Hit YouTube, Instagram everywhere your buyers hang out.
