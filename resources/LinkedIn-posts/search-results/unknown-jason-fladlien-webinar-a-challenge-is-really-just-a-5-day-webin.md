“A challenge is really just a 5-day webinar.”

 When Jason Fladlien said this at WebinarCon, it SHIFTED me.
I’ve been doing webinars.

 I’ve been doing challenges.
 But I never connected that a challenge is simply five webinars… strategically linked together to create layered transformation.
Here’s the REAL game:

👉 A webinar’s primary purpose isn’t to sell.
 (Yes, we want the revenue, but that’s not the purpose.)
 The purpose is to transform someone from ‘I can’t’ to ‘I can’ within 60–90 minutes.

Then you invite them to the next step.
But a challenge?
 Whole different level.
✨ Day 1 = Transformation.
 ✨ Day 2 = Leverage Day 1 + NEW transformation.
 ✨ Day 3 = Build again.
 ✨ You can even SELL on Day 2 (his personal preference).
 ✨ Each day stands alone—so if someone misses a day, they still get value.
 ✨ Yet the days also connect—so those who attend all 5 get the deepest breakthrough and convert the highest.

Same psychology.
Same framework.
Just extended over 5 days instead of one.

But here was the biggest truth bomb:

⚠️ If you don’t understand webinars, building a strong challenge will be HARD.
 Because a challenge is webinars—just multiplied.

Whew. Mind blown.

Not just inspiration… IMPLEMENTATION.
