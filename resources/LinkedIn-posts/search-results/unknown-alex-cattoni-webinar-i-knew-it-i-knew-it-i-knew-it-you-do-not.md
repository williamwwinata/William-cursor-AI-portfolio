Here are just a few takeaways I had to share 👇

💡 3 Secrets to Building Your Personal Brand
(+ some major misconceptions)
🚫 The Myths:
“It’s just blatant self-promotion.”
“It means sharing your private life online.”
“It’s only for experts with years of experience.”
 → Nope! The newer you are, the fresher your perspective.
“It’s too time-consuming.”

💥 Secret #1: Presence
Being present is more powerful than having tons of followers.
Pick the ONE platform your ideal clients are on
Show up — even imperfectly
Focus on connection, not perfection
➡️ Your personal brand earns compound interest.
 Followers don’t matter as much as trust does.

🔍 Secret #2: Perspective
Your perspective is your power. Choose your angle:
The Expert – teaching what you know
The Leader – “I’ve been there, here’s what worked”
The Peer – “I’m learning with you”
The Investigator – challenging outdated methods
The Curator – gathering and sharing insights from others
Your perspective can evolve over time —> just keep showing up.
 Because connection is currency, and the niche is YOU.

🗓 Secret #3: Plan
To show up consistently, you need a plan that works for you.
You don’t need to post daily —> start with once a week
Build consistency with baby steps
This is a long game —> play it with intention
🙌 Not everyone is your client, but anyone can become your referrer.

🤝 Secret #4: Accountability
It’s not daunting. Just show up —> in your way, in your time.
You don’t have to compare yourself to anyone else.
The goal isn’t perfection. It’s consistency.

✨ Content is cheap. Connection is priceless.
Remember: it takes an average of 28.87 touchpoints before a sale. Be someone your audience sees, trusts, and remembers.

So, yeah… a 3 AM webinar?    WORTH. IT.
