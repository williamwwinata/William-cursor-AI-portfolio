The real money gets made after your market says no.

This is one of those things that took me way too long to figure out.

Most people are terrified of the pitch 

That’s why they delay it, they keep adding more value, keep teaching, keep warming people up, all because they don't want to hear the word NO.

And I get it, rejection sucks, but here's the thing…

The pitch isn't the end of the conversation. 

It's actually the beginning of the real one.

Before you pitch, you're just guessing. 

You're trying to guess what they care about.

What scares them. 

What they don't understand.

But after you pitch them…

The guessing stops because now they tell you.

They don't say "I don't like your offer."

They say things like:

"I need to think about it."

"I'm not sure this will work for someone like me."

"I've tried something similar before."

"I don't have time right now."

And you shouldn’t see these as rejections.

Because these are data points.

And they only show up after the pitch.

So the goal isn’t to avoid hearing no… but to collect them 

Because every no contains the exact reason why your next webinar will convert better.

So here’s my lesson for you: 

The fastest way to make more money isn't to delay the pitch, it's to pitch sooner 

Because the sooner you pitch, the sooner you hear what's actually stopping people and the sooner you hear that, the sooner you can address it.

This is how webinars work…Sales calls… Emails… All of it.

The more no’s you get…

The more feedback you have…

And the better you become.
