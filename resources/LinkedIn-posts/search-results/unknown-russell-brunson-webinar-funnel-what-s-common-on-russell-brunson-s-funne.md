What's common on Russell Brunson's funnel?

It’s not the setup.
It’s the psychology.

After going through hundreds of his funnels, I realized something surprising
The secret isn’t a tripwire, a webinar, or a fancy upsell.
It’s trust.
Tiny pieces of it, built step by step.

He never asks for a big jump.
He just leads you one small “yes” at a time.

👉 Micro-Yes: Get the free book, it feels safe.
👉 Clarity: No shiny distractions, just YES or EXIT.
👉 Empathy: He talks about your struggle before his success.

By the time you reach the offer,
You already trust him.
You already feel understood.
So saying yes feels natural.

That’s the magic, not tricks or tools, but trust.
