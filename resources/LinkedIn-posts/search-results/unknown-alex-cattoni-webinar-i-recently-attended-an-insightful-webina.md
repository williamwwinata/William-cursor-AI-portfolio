I recently attended an insightful webinar by Alex Cattoni titled “How to Write a Conversion Story that Turns Emails into Income.”

It was a real eye-opener! The session covered everything from sourcing and saving stories to building a story inventory, understanding email goals, creating story bridges, and so much more.

What stood out the most for me were the 4 story types every writer should know:
🔹 Personal Stories make you relatable
🔹 Authority Stories build trust
🔹 Community/Client Stories show transparency and proof
🔹 Real-world Events make your message relevant

These storytelling techniques reminded me that great stories aren’t just for marketing. They are how we connect, inspire, and engage across every platform.
