Selling your business isn’t always about chasing a massive payday. Sometimes, it’s about reclaiming your life. 

For many entrepreneurs, 
the dream is to build and sell a company for millions.
But few talk about the emotional and personal toll that comes along with the journey.

For Melissa Kwan, founder of Spacio, 
selling her company was about stepping away from a business 
that no longer aligned with her personal and professional fulfillment.

This is her story, going from burnout to breakthrough, and 
how selling Spacio changed the course of her life.
