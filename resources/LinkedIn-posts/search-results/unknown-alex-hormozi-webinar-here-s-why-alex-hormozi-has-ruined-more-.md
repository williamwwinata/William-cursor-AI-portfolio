Here’s why Alex Hormozi has ruined more coaching businesses than helped.

“Value-first” is no longer enough. It arguably only worked when you had an army of social media agents under you.

And content spam? That game’s over too. Volume is only one part of a much bigger equation.

If you want to convert faster, earn trust quicker, and actually stand out stop explaining and start "demonstrating".

Workshops. Webinars. Paid live sessions. 
These aren’t just marketing tactics they are trust accelerators.

Even with a small audience, a well-structured, a low-ticket workshop can consistently outperform months of content. Because people don’t just want information they want to see what you can do.

Capability-first marketing is the new move.

Build offers that show, not just tell.

It’s worked for me (and my clients) in 2025, and will be the major focus in 2026.
