I remember watching a YouTube video of Alex Hormozi 2 years ago. In the video, he talks about how he made his first $100,000 with GymLaunch.
​
His "secret"?
​
Cold call prospects for 14-16 hours a day. 
​
That's it. 
​
So when he launched this past weekend...
​
I wasn't surprised when he went live for 10 hours...
​
Recorded over 1,000 ads...
​
And according to his webinar consultant, only slept 5 hours a day for months. 
​
That's how he has always worked.
​
Yet there are a few marketers saying that "he could've saved more time or gotten better results by doing X thing different".
​
Sure, that works if you're getting to $30k months or scaling a low-ticket offer...
​
But not for making a $80-100M launch. 
​
Us marketers are quick to try to make sense of everything that happens "out there" with something we've learned inside our little internet marketing bubble.
​
"He can't do this because X course said so." 
​
Yet Hormozi did great and his proof is in the results. 
​
(Not only that, but he had some of the best marketers in the world, including Jason Fladlien, helping him out.)
​
Anyways, great arguments from both sides, but gotta give credit where credit is due. Hormozi is a champ.
