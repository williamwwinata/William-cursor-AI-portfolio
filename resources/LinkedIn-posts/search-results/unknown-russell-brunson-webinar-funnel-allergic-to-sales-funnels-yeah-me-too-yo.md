Allergic to Sales Funnels? Yeah, Me Too.

You know the type: “free webinars” that promise value but end up being long-winded sales pitches. We’ve all been there, and let’s be real – people are tired of it.

That Russell Brunson-style marketing? It’s made us weary and wary. So when someone asks, “What are you trying to sell me?” after I genuinely reach out, I don’t take it personally.

But here’s the deal: I’m not here to manipulate anyone into buying something they don’t need. My approach is simple – add value, help people succeed, and build a stronger ecosystem.

Do I believe I can help affiliate owners and coaches grow? Absolutely. But if I have to push someone into it, they won’t succeed - and that doesn’t help anyone.

So, let’s cut to it: I don’t do hard sales. 

I’m all about real conversations and connections. 

If it makes sense to connect, amazing. If not, no hard feelings.

If you’re curious about what I offer and thinking about taking your first steps towards more freedom in your affiliate or coaching, let's grab a virtual coffee. 

No sales pitch, just a real chat.
