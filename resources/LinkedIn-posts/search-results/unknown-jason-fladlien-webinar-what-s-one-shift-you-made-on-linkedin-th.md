What’s one shift you made on LinkedIn that had the biggest impact on your visibility, influence, or opportunities?

For me... it has been always sending personalized connection request messages.

I just recorded a podcast with Joshua B. Lee on this topic... 

 If you are building a personal brand, navigating the noisy world of digital marketing, or trying to create more meaningful connections online, this is for you.

I want to highlight someone who is transforming how we show up on LinkedIn in a real and human way... 

Joshua B. Lee, the founder of Standout Authority is often called the "Dopamine Dealer of LinkedIn" and what makes him truly stand out is his belief that connection should come before conversion. 

 3 practical tips from Joshua that can transform your LinkedIn presence:

-Lead with value. Share stories and insights that educate and inspire. People connect with vulnerability and honesty, not just accomplishments.

-Engage with intention. Comment on posts thoughtfully, ask real questions, and start conversations instead of simply broadcasting.

-You are your brand. Let your personality and values shine. The more human you are, the more people will trust and remember you.
