Monday was one of those days where your brain just doesn’t stop fizzing ✨
Nearly 300 entrepreneurs in one room and speaker after speaker delivering absolute gold 🔥

Matt Hall  opening with the big questions - your goals, your why, and whether you’re actually living in alignment with them

Regina Martin being hilarious AND razor sharp on customer journeys, website design and making sure your message works for everyone

Jon Penberthy  breaking down funnels and making a seriously compelling case for webinar presentations as the ultimate lead capture and sales tool

Lunch with a room full of entrepreneurs (the networking alone was worth the ticket 😅)

Matt Hall  back with us to nail our ‘i help’ statement so people’s eyes stop glazing over when we explain what we do 🙋‍♀️

A brilliant AI panel with Phil Harrison  and Rebecca Whitney  on using it ethically and building on your knowledge base for real impact

Then Daniel Priestley  turned up and I absolutely fangirled the entire time, not sorry

Amy Atkinson  unpacking how different buyer types need completely different messaging before they’ll ever trust you enough to buy - genuinely game changing

And Lindsay McGlone  finishing the day in full sequin energy with pure Instagram gold 

Pages of notes, a very full brain, and a drive home that was pure processing time

This is why we get in the room 💛
