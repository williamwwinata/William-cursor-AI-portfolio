I have no clue why people get pissed off when others succeed.

Alex Hormozi sure pulled off one of the greatest marketing feats in history.

AND people are shitting on him!

Now, even I’m not a fan of his hustle mindset, and I don’t follow his work much.

Still, selling millions of books in a single launch deserves massive applause and tells that he knows what he's doing. 

I just watched 3+ hours of his webinar, and it’s a GREAT demonstration of:

1. Positioning
2. Mechanism
3. Storytelling

I call this the trinity to make any offer successful.

1. Positioning

Even with ads, Alex went broad and hit everyone with high-awareness symbols like “better than NFT and cheaper than Bitcoin.” 

AND the webinar opening was congruent as well: 

“Am I really gonna give everyone here who’s live a secret project I’ve been working on for four years that’s better than an NFT and less than a Bitcoin? Yes."

Even people with no clue about marketing got intrigued. He built a strong value proposition around it, and it worked.

2. Mechanism

Before revealing his money model, he stacked tons of proof.

Then he introduced the $100M Money Model equation: 30-day gross profit > 2x CAC + COGS

AND he broke it into four mechanisms anyone can use:

👉Attraction offers (get customers fast)
👉Upsell offers (increase order value)
👉Downsell offers (capture “no’s”)
👉Continuity offers (stack recurring revenue)

He taught each one through stories of his companies, backed it with proof, and showed why it works.

Even if the concepts seem basic, he owned his unique mechanism, gave it a new name, and showed how it works in practice.

3. Storytelling

This part felt meh in the beginning.

But later, especially when he shared the 10 major decisions that changed his life...it sure got emotional.

And when he talked about his wife, I thought his voice choked. That vulnerability made him relatable.

He also stacked stories that inspired people to pull out their wallets in real time.

That was indeed a powerful reminder....sometimes, even if you don’t tell great stories, stories still sell.

SO, instead of hating Hormozi, swallow a chill pill, and see if you can apply a thing or two from his stupendous success. lol
