One thing I REALLY wanted from 2026 is to work with more female leaders and woman-owned brands. 

So, I am manifesting…

Here are some women I’ve wanted to work with since I started freelancing 4 years ago.

✨ Sophie Miller

She is the marketing queen. 

Since I started my career, her carousels and newsletters have been my go-to source of information. 

If there’s one person I wanna learn from, it’s definitely her!

✨ Cassey Ho

Cassey is my childhood idol. 

As a teenager, I had severe body consciousness. 
Her videos acted as my safe haven. 

Despite being unsure about everything, I was always sure about Cassey.

So much so that I’ve followed her through the years from her YouTube channel, to blog, to clothing brand. 

And yes, along the way, I did learn a ton about marketing as well. 

✨ Alex Cattoni

There was a time in my career when I switched completely to copywriting. That’s when I found Alex. 

I learned from her posts.
Even read the newsletters.
And attended her webinars.

Those lessons still come in handy as a LinkedIn ghostwriter.

✨ Saheli Chatterjee

She is the one person I look up to during my tough times.

Her freelancing journey truly inspires me, and it’s probably the reason why I’ve reached where I am today. 

(Also, her book channel has given me tons of reading options)

✨ Lara Acosta

I discovered Lara last year when I got dead serious with my LinkedIn.

While her lessons are amazing, what stuck with me is her resilience. 
The way she handled herself through the years and rose to be an expert. 

I dream of having her confidence!

All these women have truly inspired me. 

I know I will learn so much more from them in the coming years. And hopefully work together as well!!

So if you know someone who knows someone, I don't mind if you pop a tag below.

I'm putting it out there.

What’s your top goal for 2026? I'd love to see how I can help!
