Yesterday, I tuned into Alex Hormozi’s 100M Money Models book launch and wow, what a show.
2.4M copies sold already, breaking records, and yet it felt so personal, so locked-in.

People say long webinars are dead, not when it’s Alex and Leila. You’re glued to every word.

I’ve been following Alex for 5 years now, and his frameworks always land. 100M Offers and 100M Leads shaped how I think about business and I can’t wait to dive into 100M Money Models.

Favorite reminder: “humans tend to overestimate risk and underestimate opportunity” - Jeff Bezos. The Hormozis prove again that leaning in and betting on yourself is the real unlock.

Massive gratitude for Alex Hormozi and Leila Hormozi for democratizing business education, raising standards, and showing what’s possible.
