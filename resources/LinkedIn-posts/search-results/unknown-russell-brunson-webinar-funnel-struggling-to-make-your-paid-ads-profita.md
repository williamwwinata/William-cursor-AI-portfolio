Struggling to make your paid ads profitable? 🤔 

Sending cold traffic directly to discovery call pages might be the issue. According to expert strategies, there's a better way! Instead of driving traffic straight to booking pages, consider a multi-step funnel approach. 

Start by offering a valuable free resource or low-ticket item to build trust and qualify leads. 

This way, you warm up traffic before inviting prospects to book calls, ensuring they're more likely to convert when they reach your high-ticket offer. 

Russell Brunson's methods highlight the effectiveness of adding steps like webinars or value-packed lead magnets to nurture and convert. 

This not only lowers your cost per lead but increases the value of each client, making your ad spend profitable. Ready for a shift in your strategy?
