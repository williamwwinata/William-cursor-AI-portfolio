I was paralyzed by tech. Russell's "Just Build The Damn Funnel" philosophy got me launching in 48 hours instead of overthinking for months.

Brutal Truth: A mediocre funnel that exists outperforms a perfect one in your head.

Counterintuitive Win: The more value you give, the more you earn trust (and sales).
