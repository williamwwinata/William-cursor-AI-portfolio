Here's what I mean:

❌ Generic PDF that applies to everyone
✅ Personalized quiz with custom results for each person

❌ Mini-course most people never finish
✅ Immediate, actionable insights about their specific situation

❌ One-size-fits-all webinar
✅ Tailored recommendations based on their answers
