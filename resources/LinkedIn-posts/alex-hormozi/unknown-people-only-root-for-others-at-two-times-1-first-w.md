People only root for others at two times:





1️⃣ First, when they’re at the beginning of the race.
2️⃣ Second, when they finish. 

Neither is when you need it. So, you have to master the middle. The boring, exhausting, soul-crushing middle. That’s where the winning happens. On your own.
