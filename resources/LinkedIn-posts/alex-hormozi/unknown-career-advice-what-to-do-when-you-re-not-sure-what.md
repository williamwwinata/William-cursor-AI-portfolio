Career advice: 
What to do when you’re not sure what to do:

Build capacity.

Example: 
1. Go to bed on time when you’re not sure what to do the next day
2. Build an audience when you’re not sure what product to sell
3. Add goodwill to your email list when you don’t have a promo
4. Get in shape when you don’t have dates lined up
5. Save money when you’re not sure where to invest

The thing about opportunities is that they present themselves to everyone and only people with capacity can both recognize and capitalize on them.
So don’t be in the bleachers when that fat pitch comes.

Be at the plate. Ready to swing big.
