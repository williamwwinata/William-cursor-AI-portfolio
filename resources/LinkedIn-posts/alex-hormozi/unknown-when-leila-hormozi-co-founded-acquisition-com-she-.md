When Leila Hormozi co-founded Acquisition.com, she wasn’t looking to build just “another” private equity firm. She sought out to build us into a private equity firm that operated from a place of values, integrity, and putting people first. Building private equity is fairly straightforward. Building private equity without manipulating people, undermining founders, or taking advantage of desperation many companies face is another business on its own.

“Leave everything and everyone better than you found it” is a common phrase you’ll hear around the Acquisition.com office. 

That thinking is what grew us into a firm supporting 35 portfolio companies with approximately $4 billion in enterprise value and one that helps thousands of businesses scale every year.

Today, we’re excited to announce that Leila is stepping into a role she's actually been doing all along: Executive Chairwoman.

As Executive Chairwoman, Leila will focus on long-term strategy, capital allocation, corporate governance, expansion, and the growth of all our brands. Leila’s instincts have catapulted Acquisition.com since its founding and now the business can leverage her one of a kind leadership to become the Disney of business.

To lead day-to-day operations and bring this vision, the Disney of business to life, we’re announcing Sharran Srivatsaa as our new CEO.

Sharran is a four-time Inc. 500 entrepreneur who scaled Teles Properties 10x to $3.4 billion in sales, then took The Real Brokerage from a $200 million valuation to $1.2 billion in 30 months. Having led two billion dollar businesses, Sharran clearly has the experience. However, what made him right for this role is that despite the experience, he hasn’t lost an ounce of competitive greatness no matter how many wins he’s stacked.

He’s not just a great leader, he’s a great human, a great partner, a great friend, a great husband, and a great father. He is a great example for our team, partners, and clients.

We are lucky to have two exceptional leaders steward us into the next great chapter of Acqusition.com. Leila will shape where we go and Sharran will executive how we get there.

Congratulations to Leila on this milestone, and to our new CEO, Sharran.
