Most coaches spend more time fighting with tech than actually coaching.
 That’s broken.

When I first started helping coaches with funnels, 
I noticed a painful pattern:

->They spend hours inside ClickFunnels or Kajabi.
->They copy-paste email templates that don’t sound like them.
By the time the funnel is ready… their energy is gone.

Some never even launch their webinar.

That’s when I thought:
 “What if I could build a tool that did ALL of this in 1 click?”

So that’s what I’m doing.

I’m building a SaaS that generates an entire webinar funnel + email sequence in less time than it takes to make coffee.

And I’m doing it in public.

 Follow along if you want to see the messy, honest journey of going from zero → working SaaS.

 (And if you’re a coach, you might just get early access 👀).
