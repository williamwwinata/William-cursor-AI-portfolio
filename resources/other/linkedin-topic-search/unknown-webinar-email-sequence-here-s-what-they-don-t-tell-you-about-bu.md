Here’s what they don’t tell you about building an evergreen funnel:
It works… until it doesn’t.

I had a polished webinar, a clean email sequence, and a high-converting offer.
Automated. “Passive.”

And for a while, it was magic.
Sales came in while I slept.

Until they didn’t.
Sales slowed down.
Refunds crept up.
People stopped finishing the training.
Leads felt cold.

That’s when I realized:
It’s not about the funnel itself... it’s about whether the funnel is still working for you.

Evergreen doesn’t mean “set it and forget it.”
It means “build it and then keep it alive.”

Because a funnel isn’t a machine.
It’s an ecosystem.
And ecosystems need tending. 🍃
