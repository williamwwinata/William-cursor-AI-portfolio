If you want to make $100k from a free webinar…

Let’s do the math.

→ Average webinar conversion rate is 2 to 5%

→ Let’s be generous and say you convert 5% of attendees

→ That means for every 100 people who show up, 5 will buy

If your offer is $500.

You’d need 200 sales to hit $100k.

Which means 4,000 people need to attend your webinar live.

Now factor in the average show-up rate, which sits around 30%.

To get 4,000 attendees, you’d need roughly 13,000 sign-ups.

Which means your email list needs to be massive.

Or your ad spend needs to be even bigger.

A more realistic goal if you’ve got a warm audience of 2,000 people, would be around 600 sign-ups.

Which gives you 180 attendees.

Which gives you 9 sales.

Which gives you $4,500.

It’s no wonder so many people feel stuck on the launch treadmill.

Now compare it to this:

→ 5 clients at $20k = $100k

Without writing endless emails.

Without worrying who’s going to show up live.

Just five humans.

Building you a safety net that lasts all year.

Feels different, right?
