70% of webinars get stuck under 5% conversion rates.

(It's not the webinar that is a problem.)

There are no easy ways out in marketing.

And that goes for the webinars as well.

Although they are a great conversion mechanism and can get a lot of conversions, some requirements have to be in place for them to work.

They have to be a part of a great funnel, prebuilt for success.

Webinar launches that got the most results for our clients had:

- Great landing page that qualifies leads
- Well-engineered and informative, thank you page
- Pre-webinar flow, which increases show-up rates
- Replay emails that start conversations after the webinar
- Hard sale emails that bring in the conversions

And this is just a prerequisite...

The webinar itself has to be valuable enough and solve a problem of your target audience...

It has to:

- Attack a pain point of your ideal customer
- Educate them on that problem
- When the pain is greatest, introduce a solution
- Solve it fully, so they experience true value
- Make it so that the problem solved opens up new problems

And the solution to those new problems is your offer...

This makes the difference between a webinar with 2% conversion rate and one with a 10%+ conversion rate.

For a better example, if you did a 5k on your last webinar, and didn't implement any of this...

You have the potential of doing 25k on your next one.
