What an 8-figure CEO told me last week:

"Funnels are getting crazy simple."

Couldn’t agree more.

5–10 years ago?
→ Funnels were too complex.

Socials → Lead Magnets → Webinars → Email Sequences → Low-ticket → VSLs → Upsells → Downsells → Sales calls

All just to sell a $297 course.
(On discount from $6999)


Today?
→ Most creators and coaches are adopting a 2/3-step funnel

Why?
→ Buyers are sharper.
→ Attention spans are shorter.
→ Trust builds faster when it's real.

They're meeting people where they are.
Selling directly, simply, immediately.

The old ‘value ladder’ is wobbling.


Anyone else noticing this shift?
Curious who's already testing simpler funnels right now
