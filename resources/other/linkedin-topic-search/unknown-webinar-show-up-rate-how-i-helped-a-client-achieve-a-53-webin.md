How I Helped a Client Achieve a 53% Webinar Show-Up Rate (Almost 2x the Average)

Did you know the average webinar show-up rate is only around 25 - 35%?

One of my clients crushed that benchmark with a 53% show-up rate for their latest webinar.

Here’s how we did it:
1️⃣ Dialed in the Target Audience.
We focused on quality over quantity and inviting only highly engaged leads who were genuinely interested in the topic.

2️⃣ Crafted an Irresistible Hook.
The webinar title and content promised a solution to their audience’s #1 problem, making it a “can’t-miss” event.

3️⃣ Automated a Multi-Touch Reminder Sequence.
Email reminders: Sent at registration, 48 hours before, 24 hours before, and 1 hour before.

SMS reminders: A quick text on the day of the event increased attendance by 12%.

4️⃣ Created a Smooth Registration Process.
We optimised the landing page to minimise friction and get registrations with one click and resulting in a 45% sign-up rate.

The Results:
- 53% show-up rate which is nearly double the industry average.
- Engaged attendees stayed for an average of 75% of the session.
- Warm leads ready to convert into clients immediately after the event.

If your webinars aren’t converting, it’s probably not your content or your offer, it’s your system.

It's simple.

Great system, great offer, more hot leads, more sales....
