One metric I don’t see talked about enough in event and webinar reporting: attendance conversion rate.

It goes beyond basic registration and attendance numbers. By looking at total console views and where conversions actually come from - whether that’s a partner referral or a specific location code - you start to see what’s really driving performance.

That insight changes the conversation from “How many people showed up?” to “What’s actually working, and how do we do more of it?”

When you understand which channels, partners or regions are converting best, you can:
• Optimise promotion strategy
• Improve partner-led events
• Refine targeting by geography
• Design better-performing events overall

Data like this helps teams move from guesswork to smarter decisions - and ultimately, better outcomes.
