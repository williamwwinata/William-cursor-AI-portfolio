Stop building funnel mazes for cold traffic.

You're not running a theme park.

You're trying to sell something.

But half of you treat every customer like they need a PhD to understand your water bottle.

"Let me send them through a quiz, then a webinar, then an email sequence, then maybe they'll consider purchasing."

They bounced at step one.

Your funnel options are simple:

FOR NORMAL HUMANS: 
VIDEO AD → PRODUCT PAGE → PHOTO AD (LONG COPY) → PRODUCT PAGE

That's it.

IF YOUR PRODUCT ACTUALLY NEEDS EXPLANATION: 
AD → ADVERTORIAL → PRODUCT PAGE → AD → VSL → PRODUCT PAGE

Only use these if your customers are genuinely confused without education.

Most of you don't need education funnels.

You need better offers.

Because this is what happens when you overcomplicate:

Customer clicks. 
Lands on page one of your funnel labyrinth.
"I need to watch 20 minutes to understand why I should buy vitamins?"

They leave.

You blame traffic quality.

The traffic was fine. Your funnel was ridiculous.

I've seen 7-step funnels for selling supplements.

Supplements.

We cut it to AD → PRODUCT PAGE.

Conversion rate doubled.

Your customer's attention span is 3 seconds, not 3 pages.

The best funnels don't feel like funnels.

They feel like someone showing you exactly what you want and asking if you'd like it.

Everything else is marketing masturbation.
