There's no such thing as passive sales...

Now I know that sounds funny coming from a funnel strategist. Surely that's what I'm selling here? But I'm not going to lie to you to make a sale.

And the truth is, funnels don't make sales automatic.

Here's what happens when coaches build funnels with the hopes of creating "passive income".

They build a funnel, and it works for a while.

But then the conversions drop, and instead of figuring out why, they rebuild the whole thing.

Because everyone loves more busy work. Myself included!

But after all the work of rebuilding the funnel, they often end up right back in the same place — just a month or two later.

Because funnels aren't one and done.

Markets shift. Messaging gets stale. Offers evolve.

What worked six months ago might not work today — and that's normal.

There's no such thing as passive sales. There are only sales systems you're actively paying attention to.

Some people prefer funnels. Some prefer going live. Both work. Neither is magic.

But here's what happens when a coach comes into the process with a different attitude — one where we're going to improve this little by little:

I worked with a coach who had launched an offer before at €500.

It was broad and unclear — the kind of offer where you read the sales page and think, "Wow, there's so much in here, but is it actually solving my problem? Am I going to even get through all of this? I feel like there's a lot in here that's not even relevant to me."

So we narrowed the problem and made the outcome specific. She doubled the price of the programme and scaled BACK how much support she was giving.

And this was the result:
329 webinar signups. 40% show-up rate. 15 sales. 11% live conversion.

That's what happens when you refine the offer, the messaging, and keep testing without expecting a silver bullet.

A huge increase in conversions from the last time she launched it. With a higher profit margin and lower time input from her.

Stop chasing passive income and just pick a lane — whether that's launching or funnels or whatever floats your boat. Stay consistent. Optimise what's already working.

That's how you scale.
