Coach: We're not at 7-figs yet. We need more leads.
Me: You don't actually.
Coach: How else do we grow?

Me: How many leads are you getting per month?

Coach: 2,000. 

Me: How many book a call with you?

Coach: I don't know exactly. Maybe 1%?

Me: So for every 2,000 leads you're getting 20 booked calls?

Coach: Right. That's why we need more leads.

Me: No. That's why you need to convert more of your existing leads.

Coach: But every consultant I've talked to says we need to get more leads.

Me: Of course they do. That's the easy advice. How many people can actually help you convert more? Almost none. So they tell you to get more instead.

Coach: But we're closing deals. It's working.

Me: Let me show you the math. At 1% conversion rate, you get 20 booked calls per month. At 40% close rate, you get 8 new clients. If every client is worth $3k, that's $24k/mo. Does that sound about right? 

Coach: Pretty much.

Me: Now if you were able to convert 3% of your leads into booked calls, you'd 3X your revenue without getting more leads or improving your close rate. Meaning you'd get 60 calls and 24 new clients. That would get you $72k/mo instead of $24k/mo. 

Coach: How do we actually get to 3%?

Me: What do you do after someone downloads your lead magnet?

Coach: They receive our 5-day email sequence.

Me: That's okay. But it's not enough to convert people. They need more than just emails.

Coach: What?

Me: When they finish the 5-day sequence, keep sending them newsletters 2X a week. 

Coach: What else?

Me: Are you running monthly webinars?

Coach: I've tried them but not consistently.

Me: They should be your main conversion mechanism. Cause if someone just got your lead magnet and received a couple of emails, they probably won't buy. But if they also join your webinar, your conversion rate drastically increases. One coach I worked with installed this and their lead conversion rate went from 1% to 3%. They didn't focus on getting more leads.

Coach: So you're saying we shouldn't get more leads?

Me: I'm saying lead conversion rate is your bottleneck right now. If you convert leads at 3%, you'd hit 7 figures without chasing more leads.

Coach: So what do we focus on first?

Me: Invite all of your existing leads to a webinar. Launch and test it. Once it works, run them monthly or even weekly. Invite both new and existing leads and make it a regular thing. Aim to get at least 10% of webinar attendees to book a call with you after the webinar. And your close rate will improve because they'll already come pre-sold. You can even increase your prices later on. 

Coach: And then we can worry about getting more leads?

Me: Yes, you can. But by then you probably won't need to.
