How to Use AI to Improve Landing Page Conversion

Challenge: Traffic is there, but the landing page isn’t converting.

What to Feed the AI:
Landing page URL or full copy
Offer type (eBook, call, quote, webinar, etc.)
Conversion rate
Heatmap data (optional)
Bounce rate and time on page

Prompt to Try:
“Here’s a landing page we’re using: [paste content or URL]. Based on this data and the conversion rate of X%, suggest specific edits to headline, subhead, body copy, and CTA to increase conversions. Use proven high-converting copy principles.”

AI Outcome:
You get CRO copy recommendations that are faster (and often better) than an agency’s.
