Are you posting on LinkedIn three or four times a week and getting absolutely nothing back?

You're liking posts. You're commenting. You're trying to show up. But the leads? Tumbleweeds.

Most people think the answer is to post more. It's not.

The answer is a system. Seven pillars that work together like a coordinated symphony.

Your proposition needs to be crystal clear. Your profile needs to actually sell what you do. Your content strategy needs structure - top, middle, bottom of funnel. You need to be connecting with your ideal customer profile every single day. Your engagement needs to add value, not just noise. Your ads need to amplify what already works organically. And your webinars need to convert.

Miss one of these and you're spinning your wheels.

LinkedIn is the B2B platform. If you're an SME business leader and you're not generating leads from it, something in your system is broken.
