Your content should work like compound interest. Not a lottery ticket.
Here's the framework we use at #Lebeda to turn posts into systems that multiply impact:
Step 1: Create pillar content
Write one deep piece on a core problem your customers face. For example, it's things like "Why #ransomware insurance isn't enough" or "The hidden cost of security tool sprawl."
Step 2: Break it into #microcontent
That one pillar becomes 8-10 posts. Each stands alone. Each links back to the pillar. Each drives a different angle of the same problem.
Step 3: Connect the dots
Every new post references 2-3 older posts. Your #contentlibrary becomes a #web, not a timeline. Readers go deeper instead of scrolling past.
Step 4: Repurpose with #intent
Turn posts into #casestudies. Case studies into #webinars. Webinars into email sequences. Same core message, different formats, compounding reach.
The result? Content that generates #pipeline months after we publish it. Not because it went #viral. Because it's interconnected.
Stop creating content that dies in 48 hours. Build systems that work while you sleep.
