If you're stuck at $100,000 or $150,000 as a freelance marketer, you probably don’t have enough offers…

The marketers making $400,000+ have a full product ladder with offerings at four price points.

Here's what that looks like:

$49 - Entry Product
Marketing secrets archive, industry-specific mini-course, or template pack. Create once, sell forever. Your landing page and email sequence sell it automatically.

$499 - Core Offer
Complete marketing course, website copy template kit, or quarterly strategy sessions. Use a video sales letter, webinar, or optional discovery call to sell it.

$4,999 - Premium Service
90-day implementation, StoryBrand messaging intensive, or sales funnel build. Discovery call required. You're selling yourself and your expertise, not just a product.

$49,999 - High-Ticket Partnership
Complete funnel build plus ads management, annual fractional CMO retainer, or full marketing system build-out. Multiple calls, pitch deck, custom proposal. They're buying you.

When you have offerings at each level, people move up naturally. 

They buy your $49 product, love it, want more. 

They buy your $499 course, implement it, realize they need help. 

They hire you for $4,999. 

Then they keep you on retainer for $5,000-$15,000 per month.

That's the difference between a $150,000 marketing business and a $400,000 marketing business.
