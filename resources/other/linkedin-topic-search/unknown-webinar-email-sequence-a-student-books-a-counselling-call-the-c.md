A student books a counselling call.

The counsellor spends 45 minutes discussing universities in Canada.

At the end, the student says: "Actually, my parents haven't agreed yet. And I haven't given my 12th boards."

This happens every single day in study abroad consultancies across India.

The fix isn't hiring better counsellors. It's pre-qualifying students before they ever get on a call.

Here's what that looks like in practice:

→ 𝗔 𝘀𝗵𝗼𝗿𝘁 𝗶𝗻𝘁𝗮𝗸𝗲 𝗳𝗼𝗿𝗺 at booking - target country, intake year, academic background, budget range, test scores status

→ 𝗔 𝘀𝗶𝗺𝗽𝗹𝗲 𝗹𝗲𝗮𝗱 𝘁𝗶𝗲𝗿 𝘀𝘆𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗺 - hot leads (ready in 6 months), warm leads (12 months), cold leads (just exploring)

→ 𝗗𝗶𝗳𝗳𝗲𝗿𝗲𝗻𝘁 𝗰𝗮𝗹𝗹 𝗳𝗼𝗿𝗺𝗮𝘁𝘀 𝗳𝗼𝗿 𝗱𝗶𝗳𝗳𝗲𝗿𝗲𝗻𝘁 𝘁𝗶𝗲𝗿𝘀 - not every student needs your senior counsellor for 45 minutes right now

→ 𝗔 𝗻𝘂𝗿𝘁𝘂𝗿𝗲 𝗽𝗮𝘁𝗵 𝗳𝗼𝗿 𝗲𝗮𝗿𝗹𝘆-𝘀𝘁𝗮𝗴𝗲 𝘀𝘁𝘂𝗱𝗲𝗻𝘁𝘀 - webinars, email sequences, country guides - so they come back informed and ready

The counsellor's time is your most valuable resource.

Protect it. And in doing so, you'll actually serve students better - because they'll get the right guidance at the right stage of their journey.

If you're running a study abroad consultancy and still booking calls with zero intake data - this is the single highest-leverage thing you can fix this month.
