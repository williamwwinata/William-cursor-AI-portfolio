Email marketing is super effective…

But it can also work against you.

Like for a 7-figure client I worked with last year.

He had a solid email list.

7,000+ subscribers.

And I helped him promote his webinar through email sequences to get more attendees and high-ticket sales.

The problem?

His attendance was down significantly compared to the previous year.

About 2X fewer attendees.

And I knew what the problem was…

He only sent webinar promo emails a week before the webinar.

The remaining 3 weeks of every month?

He was cold.

No emails…

Which meant no relationship building with leads.

Which meant fewer subscribers knew, liked, and trusted him.

Which led to his leads running to the competitors.

Good news, we quickly fixed the problem.

I created newsletter emails to nurture his list, handle objections, and “keep in touch.”

The result? 

He went from getting no sales on his webinars…

…To getting over a dozen attendees asking about his high ticket offers.

The offers were $997 and $2,497.

That’s the power of email marketing if you use it effectively.
