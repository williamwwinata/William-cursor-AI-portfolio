What does it mean to go evergreen from Day #1?

It's not just about creating an evergreen sales funnel in place of live launching. That's only one part of it.

Day #1 Evergreen is a mindset. 

It's you saying:

"I don't want to spend the next two years doing live webinars or taking sales calls to keep my online course from sitting on the shelf collecting dust."

And then, when you ask around about evergreen and hear words like "impossible" and "couldn't crack it"... you don't give in to the pressure to live launch.

You ask:

"What do the course creators who are making millions with their evergreen funnels have in common?"

And then you go all in on that.☝️

NOT when you've live launched X amount of times.
NOT when you've got X amount of people on your email list.

Absolutely not when you "feel ready."

If you *KNOW* your dream business doesn't demand you to be in high-energy sales mode for weeks at a time...

You don't waste time recycling someone else's "winning" live launch formula.

You go all in on evergreen from Day #1.
