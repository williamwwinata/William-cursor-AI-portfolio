Does anyone still choose a “word of the year”❓ Just me...ok.

And did you know the Fresh Start Effect is actually a real psychological phenomenon❓(Actually new news to me!)

It’s the idea that certain moments - like the start of a new year - give our brains permission to pause, reflect, and reset without needing a full life overhaul.

Probably why my Monday morning planning sessions (with myself 😜, part of my Balanced Week Blueprint) seem to work for me.

I don’t choose a long list of resolutions in the new year, but I do two things:
1.  My Clarity Compass exercise (to ground me in what I want in 4 key life areas in this season of my life/year).
2.  Choose a word - something I want to feel more of this year.

This year, my word is "pace".  

I’m learning that moving at a little slower pace (a sustainable one) and having more white space doesn’t have to mean falling behind.  It actually helps get me to my heath and other goals faster 😁

What's your word?  What do you want to feel more (or more often) in 2026?
