The “ping” of the holiday rush hit earlier than usual this year for me. Everywhere I looked: Black Friday, Cyber Monday, last chance, don’t miss it.

And then I was asked if all my Christmas shopping was done (on Dec 1st!) - and the pressure of the "things to do" started mounting.

And honestly? It reminded me of what I wanted to actually call into this season...but it will take intentionality:
✨ To slow down.
✨ To savor.
✨ To choose presence over pressure.

Here’s my best practical advice - and what I’m sharing with the working moms I support this season.
