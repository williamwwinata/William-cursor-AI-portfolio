Stop chasing cold leads when warm prospects are raising their hands.

Most B2B sellers ignore these intent signals:
→ LinkedIn profile visits on your page
→ Likes/comments on your posts
→ Email opens and clicks
→ Pricing page visits
→ Case study downloads
→ Webinar registrations
→ Demo page abandonment

These people are showing interest.
Yet you're cold-calling strangers from purchased lists?

Here's what smart sellers do:
1. Track who engages with your content
2. Score leads based on intent signals
3 . Prioritize high-signal prospects
4. Reach out while they're warm

Prospects with 3+ intent signals are 5x more likely to convert.
Your warm audience is telling you they're interested.
