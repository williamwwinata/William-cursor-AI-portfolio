As an online coach, what do you have to do to make ₹10 Lakh of Revenue?

Let's assume,

Your course or program is priced at - ₹10,000/-
You need 100 Sales to Make ₹10L
CPL for your Lead Magnet (Free Webinar) - ₹50/-

You need to generate 5000 Leads of which 2% of People will become your customer to have 100 sales.
2% of Overall 5000 Leads = 100 Customer
For your webinar, Show up rate - 35%
People in the webinar = 1750

At 4% Webinar Conversion = 70 Sales

With 4 Team Members Support of Replay + Follow up + Calls = 30 Sales More
You can implement this strategy over months and see the CASH FLOW in your online business.
