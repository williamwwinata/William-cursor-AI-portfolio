Once you’re a marketer, I swear it’s impossible to step away…

This is literally what’s happening right now 

Wait for it…

So we had a baby boy at 1am last night. 

Fell asleep on the couch in the hospital room about 5am

Woke up an hour ago, and my wife asked me to get some coffee and a muffin. 

I found a cool place called HoundstoothATX about 3/4 of a mile away. 

Weather is really nice today… cool air, warm sun, definitely feels like fall. 

I walk in to order a coffee and the barista says congratulations!

Sorry?

You just had a baby I assume?

Yeah, how’d you know? …and he points down at my arm (see photo)

Nice dude and cool spot. 

Now, here’s how marketing follows me around for some reason. 

Before walking back, I decided to sit down outside to have a little coffee and just kind of enjoy life and the moment I’m in right now. 

…at the table next to me there are two guys in their mid 20s chatting. 

And what are they talking about?

Dude, the guy is selling the other guy a bizopp. 

The guy selling has only been in the program two months and is selling another guy

“My best friend is the one who got me into it. At first he started inviting me to events, and I realized how much money I could make.” (Direct quote)

“So are you religious? Don’t believe in a higher power or maybe you’re just spiritual?”

Then I hear…

- Free webinar funnel 
- Multiple courses 
- Multiple offers
- One on one coaching 

“I teach people to think bigger. What I help people realize is they are destined for more. 

I helped this one woman who only thought she could make 100 make 250.”

Maybe it’s not that marketing follows me around, but rather…

I just have an ear for Upsell Coming 😃
