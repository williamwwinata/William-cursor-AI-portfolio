When's the last time you paid for a movie ticket, saw something you didn't like in the first 5 minutes, and left?

Never.

Here's why that matters for your business.

You've probably watched a movie trailer and decided not to go.

You didn't pay anything, so walking away was easy.

But once you've bought the ticket, driven to the theater, got your popcorn?

You're staying. Even if the movie is mid.

Because you paid.

This is human psychology:

People who pay, pay attention.

People who pay attention, pay more.

Free webinars get 30% show-up rates.

Paid webinars get 80%+ show-up rates.

Same content. Different commitment.

But here's the catch:

Most people won't pay for something they don't understand or trust yet.

This is where your email list comes in.

Your emails build the trust needed for someone to pay attention.

They educate. They demonstrate value. They prove you're worth the investment.

Then when you ask them to pay—whether it's for a product, a presentation, or their time—they show up.

Because you've already made deposits into the relationship.

Stop giving everything away for free, hoping people will pay attention.

Build a list. Nurture it. Then ask for commitment.
