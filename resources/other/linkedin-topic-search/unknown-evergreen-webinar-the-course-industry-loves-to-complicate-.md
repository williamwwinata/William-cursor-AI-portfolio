The course industry loves to complicate things.
15-email sequences.
7 upsells.
Multiple lead magnets.
Evergreen webinars.
Monthly live launches.

But from my experience?
You can sell your course with one clear offer, one simple funnel, and a system that doesn’t burn you out.

Start with this question:
How will people find it, trust it, and buy it?

Then build:
→ A simple lead magnet
→ A welcome sequence that builds trust
→ A sales page that clearly communicates the transformation
→ A short email launch (or pre-sale page) to test your idea

Simple sells.
Especially when you're still testing and learning.
