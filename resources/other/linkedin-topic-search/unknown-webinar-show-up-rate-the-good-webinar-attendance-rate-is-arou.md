The good webinar attendance rate is around 50% or higher.
​
If you are a coach and your webinar show-up rate is low, then you must optimize, otherwise, you’re wasting ad spend and leaving sales on the table.
​
From my past projects building webinar funnels for coaches, one of the important factor that improves attendance isn’t just reminders… it’s pre-frame nurturing.
​
Why pre-frame nurturing works? Because most people register for a webinar, get busy, and forget.
​
If the first time they “hear from you” is the day of the webinar, there’s no emotional connection.
​
Pre-frame nurturing changes that. It warms up your audience before the webinar starts so that by the time the event goes live, they’re already looking forward.
​
Here's how I help clients pre-frame effectively
​

 Send short value-packed tips: Give them a quick win related to your webinar topic.
​
 Share personal stories: Let them know who you are and why this topic matters to you.
​
Spark curiosity: Hint at what you’ll reveal during the webinar without giving it all away.
​
This way, every email, video, or message builds anticipation, and when the live session starts, they feel like they must be there.
​
One action you must avoid:
Don’t just send plain “reminder” emails with the date and link. 

​
Because when you only remind without pre-framing, people treat your webinar like a calendar notification...
​
But when you nurture, you build desire and connection… so they want to show up, not just “remember to.”
​
That’s how I help my DFY funnel clients: not only creating registration pages but building a system that turns cold registrants into warm, excited attendees.
