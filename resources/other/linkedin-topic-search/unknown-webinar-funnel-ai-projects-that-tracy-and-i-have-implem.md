AI projects that Tracy and I have implemented…

KPI Tracker - this has each person’s unique KPI’s on the team. It pulls the data, calculates, and shows where they are on their weekly / monthly / quarterly KPIs. Plus there is report function for when we have 1 on 1’s with each person. 

Webinar Funnel Intelligence App - this collects all data from Facebook ads, clickfunnels, Demio, and active campaign to determine which stage of funnel is working (or not working) to recommend changes, tests, and the next campaign. It guides us on how to build Facebook Ads. 

Probate Project Manager - we have LOTS of probate cases. It’s a science of what to work on next with…race to probate the matter, statutory filings, plus big milestones that “unlock” more work. This tells the team exactly what to do and when.
