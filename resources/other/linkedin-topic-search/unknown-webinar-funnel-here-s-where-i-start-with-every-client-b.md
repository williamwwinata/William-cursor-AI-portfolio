Here’s where I start with every client before touching a single funnel or ad.

I run a deep analysis across four areas:

Offer Stack – What are you actually selling? Do you have a low-ticket, mid-ticket, and high-ticket offer? What’s the customer journey between them?

Conversion Mechanism – How are you actually converting traffic into buyers? Are you using a VSL Funnel, A Webinar Funnel, A Low Ticket Funnel?

Traffic Sources – Are you driving traffic through content (LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube) or through paid media? Is it consistent and scalable?

Funnel & Messaging – Do you have the right messaging for your offer? A B2B business with a sophisticated market, should not be speaking to their audience the same way that a Amazon FBA coach who targets young professionals targets their audience 

This is the foundation.

When I ran this process for one of my clients, he made $45K in one day through a webinar funnel. Then over $45K more over the next week

Because he didn't have the right conversion mechanism for his offer (we changed it from a VSL funnel to a webinar funnel)
