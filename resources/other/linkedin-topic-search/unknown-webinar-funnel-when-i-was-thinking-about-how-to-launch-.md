When I was thinking about how to launch my book, the usual playbook didn’t sit right. I also had zero money to spend on any marketing.

Then one day, on a run, the idea hit me hard:
 
What if the book wasn’t the focus?
 
What if it was the spark for something bigger?

I wanted to gather as many people as I could around a cause I care deeply about—and give back in the process.

That’s how the Run/Walk for Refugees was born.
 
What started as a simple idea became a small movement in the community.

This is the story of how I packed my book launch with t-shirts, purpose, and a whole lot of heart.

To me, this is what conscious marketing looks like.
 
Using your platform to do something that actually matters.

All inside this week’s Rising Authorpreneurs.

Enjoy the ride.
