Recently, I had the chance to review a marketer’s experience with webinar promotion in the B2C space. One of the channels used was Facebook Ads — supported by closed groups and a broader community strategy.

Naturally, digital brings in a colder audience, which often leads to lower webinar show-up rates.

It made me think: if you’re paying for digital leads, expecting them to convert right at the webinar stage might not be the best strategy. A two-step journey — warm-up content, lead nurturing, or value-building before and after the event — might be more effective.

📌 As someone not active on Facebook personally since 2022, I’m curious how relevant it still is as a touchpoint in people’s lives.
