I'll say it: for most businesses, automated webinar funnels are a TRAP.

(Trust me… we have ran them for YEARS but 2025 we saw them die a painful death 💀)

The allure of a sales machine running 24/7 for many is too good to resist.

But the reality is CONSUMPTION becomes the biggest problem.

It’s easy to get people to opt-in… 

…but very hard to get them to consume enough of the content.

Is it the TikTok-ification of attention spans?

Dare I say Temu-ification?!!

Maybe.

What I do know is there is a real MAGIC to running live webinars and events.

People show up and people consume when it’s live.

But if they get one sniff that it’s ‘recorded’... poof. Engagement evaporates.

It’s why we are ‘all-in’ on live events this year… 👍
