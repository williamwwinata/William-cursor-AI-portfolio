Your Meta Ads Manager says 50 leads.
Your CRM says 35 leads.
Your sales team says 20 are qualified.

This isn't a lead quality problem. It's a data problem.

Inaccurate data is the silent killer of ad campaign scalability. If you can't trust your numbers, you're flying blind.

Here’s a quick diagnostic for your tracking health. You might have a problem if:

▪️ There's a >10% discrepancy between platform-reported conversions and your ground truth (CRM/spreadsheet).
▪️ Your agency can't clearly explain your current Conversions API (CAPI) setup and event match quality score.
▪️ You make budget decisions based purely on what the Ads Manager dashboard tells you.
▪️ You aren't tracking post-lead metrics (e.g., webinar show-up rate, application rate) and feeding that data back into your decision-making.

Weak tracking means Meta optimises for the wrong signals.
You waste money on audiences that *look* good on paper but never convert to actual revenue for your coaching program or institute.

Predictable growth doesn't come from guessing.
It comes from a system of measurement so robust that every decision is backed by clean, reliable data.

Clarity isn't a luxury; it's the bedrock of performance.
