Most funnels fail before they even launch.

Use AI the right way to convert.

Justin shows you how:

1. Pick the Right Funnel Type

- Squeeze = emails.
- Sales = deep pitch.
- Webinar = signups.

Wrong funnel = wasted traffic and time.

2. Know the Essential Elements
Headline and subheadline must hook fast.
Benefits + social proof drive belief.

3. Adjust Length by Offer Complexity
High-ticket = more words, more trust.
Complex offers need detailed walkthroughs.

4. Follow Simple Design Rules
Use F-pattern layout and CTA above fold.
Stick to 3 colors, 2 fonts max.

5. Keep it Skimmable
Short sections, bullets, and image breaks.
Readers scan first - then they read.

6. Visually Engage Visitors
Bold colors, high-contrast images, clear actions.
Design should direct attention, not distract.

7. Leverage AI Funnel Builders
Try Gamma AI (drag-and-drop).
Or SiteKick, Durable, GoHighLevel.

8. Test & Optimize with AI
Use AI to detect drop-offs.
Fix weak points. Boost conversions.

Build smarter, not slower.

AI helps - but only with the right playbook.

Funnels don’t convert by accident.
