🎯 Ever feel like your webinar data is scattered across 5+ tools... with zero visibility into what actually drives revenue?

I just automated the entire webinar funnel using Zapier, and the results speak for themselves. 

𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗣𝗿𝗼𝗯𝗹𝗲𝗺: 
- Registrations coming from HubSpot, LinkedIn Lead Gen, Google Forms → UTMs lost, duplicates everywhere 
- Replay viewers invisible to Sales → Missing our highest-intent leads 
- Ad spend in one place, conversions in another → Can't calculate real CPL or ROI 

𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗦𝗼𝗹𝘂𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻: 
Built a fully automated system that: 
✅ Normalizes ALL registration sources → Creates single HubSpot contact → Registers in Zoom → Sends confirmation with join link 
✅ Tracks on-demand engagement (% watched, CTA clicks) → Scores leads → Alerts Sales on high-intent behavior 
✅ Unifies ad spend + registration + attendance + pipeline data → Real CPL, CPA, and Meeting/SQL rates by campaign 

𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗥𝗲𝘀𝘂𝗹𝘁: 
- Clean CRM with perfect attribution - Sales following up on engaged replay viewers within hours 
- Dashboard showing true ROI per webinar and per campaign 
- Scalable system that gets easier with each new webinar 

The best part? Once the automation is built, replicating it for future campaigns is effortless.
