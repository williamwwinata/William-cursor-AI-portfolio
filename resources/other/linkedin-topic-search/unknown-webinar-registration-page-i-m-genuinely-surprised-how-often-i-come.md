I’m genuinely surprised how often I come across test plans where teams don’t clearly differentiate between Performance Testing, Load Testing, and Stress Testing — even at the SME level. These are not just buzzwords; they serve completely different purposes in ensuring your web application runs smoothly, scales under real user load, and gracefully recovers under pressure. If your team is lumping them all together, it’s time for a serious testing strategy refresh. 🚨 Let’s stop checking boxes and start testing with intent. #PerformanceTesting #LoadTesting #StressTesting #WebApp #QualityEngineering #TechLeadership
