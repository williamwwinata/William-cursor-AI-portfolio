Producer / Marketer for an online school

Scope of Responsibility:
Full marketing and product transformation of the online school: strategy, product line, sales funnels, launches, content, Telegram ecosystem, and team management.

Key Tasks:
Comprehensive audit of the online school’s marketing funnel and business processes
Development of a new marketing and product strategy for the online school
Full rebranding of the online school: positioning, website, landing pages, communications
Restructuring of the product line and launch of new products from scratch (in collaboration with a methodologist)
Design and implementation of an automated webinar sales funnel (registration → webinar → sale)
Development of webinar scripts and sales presentations
Setup of paid and organic traffic systems (YouTube, YAN, landing pages)
Telegram Marketing and Launches:
Development of Telegram strategy as the main channel for nurturing and sales
Management of the school’s main Telegram channel: content strategy, nurturing, sales
Creation and management of private Telegram channels for specific products and cohorts
Development of nurturing and sales sequences within Telegram
Setup of Telegram bots: registration, material delivery, automated funnels
Audience segmentation and communication across funnel stages
Development of content strategy and database engagement strategy
Reorganization of the sales department: scripts, training, coaching
Management of team and contractors (copywriters, SMM, designers, traffic specialists)
Testing of marketing hypotheses and funnel conversion optimization

Results:
Full rebranding and restructuring of the online school (products, website, funnels, positioning)
Launch of a new product and delivery of an 8M RUB launch (previous launches ~3M RUB)
Implementation of automated webinar and Telegram sales funnels
Webinar attendance rate increased from 30% to 78%


Telegram established as the main channel for launch nurturing and sales
Creation of a system of launch and private Telegram channels
Launch of a YouTube channel and development of a stable organic traffic flow into the funnel

Reactivation of the audience base and LTV growth through content strategy and nurturing
Restructuring of the sales system: scripts, manager training, conversion growth
Establishment of a systematic marketing structure instead of fragmented processes
