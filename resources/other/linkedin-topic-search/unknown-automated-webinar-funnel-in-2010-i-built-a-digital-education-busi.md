In 2010, I built a digital education business from zero.
No templates. No SaaS tools. No playbook.
Just strategy, instinct, and a lot of manual work.
Here's what we built — and what it would look like today.

Then (2010–2015):
We launched a personal development online school in Moscow.
Full product suite. Automated webinar funnels. Email sequences.
Lead magnets connected to paid programmes.

A 2-person team running what felt like a much bigger operation.
It worked. But it was hard.
Every email sequence was written and scheduled manually.
Every webinar follow-up was copy-pasted.

Audience segmentation? Done by hand in spreadsheets.
We were doing automation — we just didn't have the tools to make it effortless.

Now (2025–2026) — what AI changes:
→ AI-powered email personalisation (e.g. ActiveCampaign AI, Klaviyo) lifts open rates from an industry average of ~22% to 35–40%. Same list, better results.

→ Webinar funnel automation with tools like Systeme.io or GoHighLevel reduces follow-up time from hours to minutes. Industry data shows automated post-webinar sequences convert 18–25% of attendees into buyers vs ~10% with manual follow-up.

→ AI content generation  cuts content production time by 60–70%. A launch campaign that took 3 weeks to write now takes 4–5 days.

→ Predictive lead scoring identifies your hottest leads before you spend time on them. Average improvement in sales conversion: 20–28% (Salesforce State of Marketing, 2024).

The strategy we used in 2010 was correct.
The execution just needed better tools.

That's the gap AI closes in 2026.
If you're running an education business or any product-based funnel today — and you're still doing this manually — you're leaving real money on the table.
Not because you're doing it wrong.
Because the tools now exist to do it faster, with less effort, and more consistently.
