60% of B2B marketers say their content drives zero pipeline.

That's not a budget problem. That's a strategy problem.

I've seen teams spend $50K+/year on blogs, webinars, case studies and not track a single sourced deal back to it.

Here's what the data actually shows:

→ Case studies close deals faster when tied to specific pain points.
→ Thought leadership drives ICP awareness, not always pipeline, but always presence.
→ Webinars work when the follow-up sequence is killer, not the webinar itself.
→ Email sequences are the dark horse - highest ROI when personalized.

The real question isn't “what content should we create?”

It’s “which content moves buyers at each stage of the funnel?”

I want to know what's actually working for you..
