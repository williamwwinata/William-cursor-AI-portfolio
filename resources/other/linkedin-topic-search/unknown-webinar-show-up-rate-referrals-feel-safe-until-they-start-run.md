Referrals feel safe.

Until they start running your practice.

They decide when work comes in.

They decide who you work with.

And most conversations start with one line: “What’s your fee?”

That’s not growth.

That’s dependency.

In this week’s newsletter, I break down why referral-only practices eventually stall and what progressive CAs build instead to create:

✅ predictable clients

✅ cleaner scopes

✅ calmer weeks

✅ and the confidence to charge 30–50% higher fees without negotiating.

If you’ve ever felt busy but replaceable, this edition is for you.
