Why focusing on show up rate can transform your sales.

Generating leads is just the start.

The real challenge kicks in when those leads are not showing up for calls, webinars, or consultations.

→ Show up rate is crucial
It's the invisible bottleneck in the sales process. If it isn't tracked and optimized, all other efforts are wasted.

→ Identify the drop off
Understanding exactly where potential customers are dropping off is key. It could be right after they agree or before the event. Pinpoint the friction point.

→ Add social proof in reminders
Including a testimonial in your reminders can make a difference. Simple phrases like, "I almost skipped this call but I’m so glad I didn’t," shift hesitation into participation.

→ Automate follow ups smartly
Not everyone responds to the same channel. Use automation to send reminders through different mediums if the original isn't opened.

→ Test everything
From the subject lines of your emails to the wording in your links, small changes can lead to big improvements.

An effective show up rate turns potential losses into wins, leveraging the whole marketing engine's value.

P.S. Looking to boost sales without extra lead generation?
Focus on improving your show up rate.
