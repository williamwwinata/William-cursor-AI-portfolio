Here are the 3 tools helping our coaching clients scale to multiple 6-figures:

1. Manychat
→ Automates comment responses
→ Builds organic engagement sequences
→ Pre-qualifies leads through DMs

2. Go High Level
→ All-in-one CRM that delivers
→ Hosts entire course libraries
→ Tracks every client interaction

3. Webinars
→ Significantly increases conversion rates
→ Perfect for building trust with paid traffic
→ Creates content from recordings

But here's what we tell every coach we work with...

These tools mean nothing without a proven system to make them work.

We've seen too many coaches buy fancy tech stacks because their competitors have.

The coaches crushing it in our agency?

They focused on the basics first.

Then let these tools amplify what was already working.

That's how we help them build sustainable coaching businesses.

Remember: Tools don't build businesses. Results do.
