I made $14k in the last 3 months using LinkedIn.
If I had to rebuild my business from scratch in 2025, 
here’s EXACTLY what I’d do:

Step 1: Clear Messaging

Write down on a piece of paper how does your ideal client look like where are they from what do they do, what do they hate what do they fear and what do they want.

Step 2: One Ultimate Offer
Based on your ideal client avatar solve one problem that is keeping them
awake at night, this becomes your high ticket offer.

(If you want me to create your offer, comment "Offer" and I'll send you the next steps) 

Step 3: Create Content That Converts.
Now that your offer is created, its time you base your content on that so
- Bust myths they already believe
- Speak to emotional pain points
- Tell stories that connect with their pain and desires

Step 4: Simplify Your Funnel.
I’d use our Client Attraction Funnel to generate leads on repeat 
without webinars, email sequences, or hustle culture.
Simple. Intentional. Scalable.

That's how I built my 6-figure coaching business.
