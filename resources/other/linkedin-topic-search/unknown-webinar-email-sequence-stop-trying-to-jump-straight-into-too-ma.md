Stop trying to jump straight into "𝙖𝙪𝙩𝙤𝙢𝙖𝙩𝙞𝙤𝙣"!

Too many companies jump straight to automated LinkedIn outreach, evergreen webinars, and email sequences without validation.

𝗛𝗲𝗿𝗲'𝘀 𝘄𝗵𝗮𝘁 𝘃𝗮𝗹𝗶𝗱𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗮𝗰𝘁𝘂𝗮𝗹𝗹𝘆 𝗺𝗲𝗮𝗻𝘀:

Test for 30-90 days (sometimes longer) to establish your baseline. You need to know that AD1 with this copy and graphic, EMAIL1 with this subject line, and LANDING PAGE1 with this headline actually convert.

Even the button color matters. I joke about it, but it really does.

The point is: establish a control. Know what works before you automate it.

Your budget and effort determine how long testing takes, but skipping this step is why most marketing campaigns fail.

Validate first. Automate second.
