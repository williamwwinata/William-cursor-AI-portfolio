$10 million ARR. Fully bootstrapped. In a market ChatGPT said was impossible. 

I sat down with Niels Klement, CMO at Perspective, to unpack how they bootstrapped a $10M ARR SaaS in four years off the back of a webinar funnel and a very specific market trend almost everyone slept on.

We went deep on funnels, paid acquisition, and the boring-but-brutal truth about what actually moves the needle in growth.

We covered a lot.

…Why SaaS is inherently cashflow-ugly, how Perspective used yearly-plan webinars to front-load cash, why mobile-first funnels quietly outperform “pretty” desktop builds, and how a niche use case in recruiting became their beachhead to dominate the DACH market.

We also broke down Neils’ creative engine...how his team thinks about concepts vs iterations, why copy is the real leverage in ads, and how to decide what to focus on when everything feels important.

What you’ll learn:
- Why SaaS cashflow breaks when you rely on low-ticket monthly plans
- How a simple webinar funnel can unlock paid ads at scale
- How a recruiting use case took Perspective to $10M ARR in DACH
- Why mobile-first, interactive funnels beat desktop-first funnel builders
- How Neils structures a performance marketing team around creative
- The difference between new concepts vs tiny iterations in ad creative
- Why the best copy starts with customer context, not frameworks
- How to think about constraints, growth planning, and where to focus
