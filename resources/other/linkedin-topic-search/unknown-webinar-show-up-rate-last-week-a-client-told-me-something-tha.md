Last week, a client told me something that I hear more often now.

“Ajit, our webinar show-up rate is below 20%.
 Maybe webinars just don’t work anymore.”


I stopped him right there.
Because what’s happening right now isn’t that webinars stopped working.

It’s that the old webinar playbook stopped working.

Back in 2023 and even 2024, things were simpler.


You could run ads on Meta, hit the right pain point, and registrations would come in fast.

 A couple of reminder emails… maybe one SMS… and people actually showed up.

But 2026 behaves very differently.


People still register.

But when it's time to attend,
 That’s where most funnels collapse.

And this is where many coaches and course creators misread the situation.

They start fixing the wrong things:
 The ads.
 The landing page.
 The targeting.

But after reviewing multiple webinar funnels recently, the real issue shows up somewhere else.


Follow-up.

Most campaigns today put:
 80% effort into getting registrations
 and barely any effort into nurturing those registrants.

That imbalance is expensive.

Because attention is harder to win now.

People need to be:
 Reminded
 Re-engaged
 Emotionally pulled back into the session


Multiple times before the webinar even starts.

In fact, the campaigns that still get strong show-up rates today usually invest far more effort in nurturing than in acquiring the lead itself.

So if your webinar room looks half empty lately…

You might not be running a bad funnel.

You might just be running a 2023 strategy in a 2026 market.

Curious to hear from others running webinars right now:

Are you seeing the same drop in show-up rates?
