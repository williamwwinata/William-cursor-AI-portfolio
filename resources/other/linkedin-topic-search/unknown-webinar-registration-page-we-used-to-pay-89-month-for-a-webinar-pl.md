We used to pay $89/month for a webinar platform. Until I realized… I already had one.

If you're subscribed to Microsoft 365, there's a good chance you're sitting on a hidden gem: Microsoft Teams for Webinars.

Yep — Teams isn't just for internal meetings.
You can run full-scale webinars:
• registration pages
• waiting rooms
• attendee insights
• screen sharing & Q&A
• even branded emails

And the best part? No extra cost — it's included in most business plans.
So before signing up for yet another monthly tool, double-check what your Microsoft license actually covers.
You might already have what you need.

Small savings add up. Smart tools matter.
