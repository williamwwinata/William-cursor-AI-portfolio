What do you do when your demand gen team is expected to produce content AND generate pipeline — with no dedicated content function?

This is one of the most common situations I hear from marketing leaders right now. Lean teams. Big expectations. Not enough hours in the day.

Original research is one of the highest-leverage investments a resource-constrained team can make because one project does the work of many.

A single research report can fuel:
✅ Paid lead gen campaigns for months
✅ PR pitches to trade publications
✅ Sales enablement assets for new reps
✅ Social content for an entire quarter
✅ Blog posts, webinars, email sequences

You're not producing one piece of content. You're producing the source material for your entire content strategy.

And the process doesn't have to be burdensome. Done right, your team's total time investment is less than 10 hours. The right research partner handles everything else.

If your team is stretched thin and trying to figure out where to focus, this is worth thinking about.
