Your webinar registration page is probably converting at 10-15% when it should be hitting 59%. 

After analyzing 500,000 webinar registrants and surveying 227 B2B marketers, we discovered exactly what separates high-converting registration pages from the rest.

Key stats from our webinar stats report: 

- 59% conversion rate strategies vs industry average 10-15%
- Thursday webinars get 30% more registrations than any other day
- 78% of registrations happen on desktop, 22% on mobile
- 90-minute webinars get the highest attendance rates (but fewer registrations)
- 45% of successful marketers use guest speakers for every webinar

So, turns out small changes like when you host, who’s on stage, and how you design the page make the biggest difference.
