At one point, I had tried everything:

 • New CRM
 • Email list builder
 • Workshops and webinars
 • “One funnel away”
 • High-ticket, low-ticket, no-ticket

And guess what?
Nothing worked the way I hoped.

Because tools don’t fix broken strategy.

Tactics don’t solve unclear offers.

And motion doesn’t mean momentum.

The truth?
It wasn’t effort I lacked. It was focus.

Once I stepped back and got honest about what was actually working…
Once I stopped spinning and started simplifying…
Everything changed.

Sometimes the breakthrough comes not from doing more...

But from doing less, better.

So I’ll ask you what I had to ask myself:

What’s the ONE thing that, if you did it consistently, would make everything else easier or irrelevant?

Start there.
