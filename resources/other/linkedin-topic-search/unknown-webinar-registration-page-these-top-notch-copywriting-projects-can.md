These Top Notch Copywriting Projects Can Change Your Life

💰 Average Rates by Project Type

1) Long-Form Sales Pages

Freelance Rate: $1,500 → $15,000+ per page

Agencies / Top Earners: $10,000 → $50,000+ (for high-revenue launches)

📌 Reason: These are high-impact conversion assets that directly affect revenue.

2) Email Marketing Sequences

Per Email: $250 → $1,500

Full Sequence (5–10 emails): $1,500 → $7,500+

💡 Top writers often charge per sequence, not per hour.

3) Landing Pages & Lead Magnets

Landing Page Copy: $800 → $5,000

Lead Magnet (e-book/guide) + Page: $1,000 → $7,500

4) Paid Ad Copy (Social & Search)

Ad Set (3–6 versions): $300 → $1,500

High-end campaigns (strategic + testing): $1,500 → $5,000+

📌 Some copywriters charge per platform (Facebook vs. Google vs. TikTok).

5) Video Script Copy

Short Ads (15–60 sec): $500 → $3,000

VSLs (10–30 minutes): $2,000 → $15,000+

💡 VSLs are among the most lucrative when done well.

6) Webinar & Event Promotion

Registration page: $800 → $3,000

Email sequence: $1,200 → $6,000

Webinar script: $1,500 → $10,000+

7) Ecommerce Product Descriptions

Per product: $30 → $250

Collections / Category Pages: $200 → $1,500

💡 Many copywriters bundle these as part of a full launch package.

8) Funnel Strategy + Copy

Basic Funnel (3 steps): $2,500 → $10,000

Complex Funnel (5+ steps): $7,500 → $30,000+

🧠 Strategic thinking commands premium pricing.

9) Direct Mail Campaigns

Letter or Postcard Copy: $500 → $2,500 each

Full Campaign (3-piece): $2,000 → $10,000+

10) SMS/Text Marketing

Per message: $50 → $300

Bundle of Messages: $500 → $3,000+
