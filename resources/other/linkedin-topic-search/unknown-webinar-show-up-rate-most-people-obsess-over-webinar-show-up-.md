Most people obsess over webinar show-up rates.

“How do I get more people to attend live?”

Fair question.
 
Wrong place to focus.

Some of your best buyers will never attend your webinar.

Not because they’re uninterested.
 
Because they’re busy, successful, and time-poor.

In my last launch I stopped relying on the webinar to do all the selling and instead qualified people before the event.

Result:
£30k in sales before I even delivered the webinar.

• Why the most motivated buyers often skip live events
• How to identify your “gold group” before the webinar
• The simple Turbo Call strategy that turns intent into sales
