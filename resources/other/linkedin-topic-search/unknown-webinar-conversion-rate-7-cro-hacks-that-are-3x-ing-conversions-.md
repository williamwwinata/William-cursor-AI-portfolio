🚀 7 CRO Hacks That Are 3X-ing Conversions in EdTech Right Now! 🚀

1️⃣ Segmented Comms: +28% conversions when you send the right nudge to the right parent/student at the right time.
2️⃣ Mobile-First UX: 67% of Indian learners are mobile-only. A 1-sec faster site = 7% more signups!
3️⃣ Show Real Results: 81% trust brands with student success stories. Video testimonials = instant credibility.
4️⃣ Live Demos/Webinars: 15 %+ conversion rates when prospects interact with real teachers, not just bots.
5️⃣ CRM Automation: 300% higher conversions with automated follow-ups and reminders.
6️⃣Simple Forms: 4%+ pop-up conversion if your form is short, sweet, and mobile-friendly.
