Most people think launching a digital course is about “recording videos.”

Absolutely Wrong.

Here’s the real 8-step framework that actually sells:

- Make key decisions
- Validate the idea
- Pre-sell to founding members
- Create the outline
- Craft an irresistible offer
- Build sales + webinar pages
- Write email sequences
- Launch, then record

Execution > perfection.
