Our simple strategy allowed our client to fund their business for nearly one year and to take a well-deserved break. 🏖️

Our client had a precise goal in mind and this is what we did to make it happen…

First of all we mapped out a full-scale marketing strategy including:
🖥️ Live and evergreen webinars
💻 Facebook & IG ad campaigns
📧 Email marketing

1️⃣ From concept to creation, we scripted, filmed, edited and developed the evergreen webinar for the client. We also planned and executed the live webinar.

These webinars were designed to introduce the client’s target audience to the teaching style and nurture them about the content in the upcoming course.

2️⃣ The client had an audience of people who took the course last year that had potential to return. So, to maximize revenue, the strategy also included a limited $7k pay-in-full upsell option (an advanced level) limited to 20 seats.

3️⃣ As we were planning the event, we ran targeted ads to generate email sign ups from the ideal audience.

Once the events were set up, we used Facebook & IG ads to drive traffic to the webinars, sent invitations and reminders to the email list.

The results after implementing this strategy are incredible!
🔴 $315k in immediate revenue
🔴 $669k projected with payment plans
🔴 323 seats filled → 3x the original goal

Not to mention all of the attention she generated and the lifetime value of each person with the future courses they’ll likely attend…

To achieve this we spent $42k on ads → 15x ROI🔥
