I Just wrapped up this Email Marketing Performance Dashboard on Looker Studio! for Kable Media, designed to turn data into decisions!

Here’s what stood out:
• 1,277 conversions were driven by over 22,000 opened emails, showing clear audience engagement.

• Top-performing countries? India, Nigeria, and Canada. These markets are worth deeper targeting in future campaigns.

• Sign-up CTAs led the pack with the highest click-through rate at 40.1%, proving that simplicity and clarity in messaging work.

• Lead nurturing campaigns held their ground, but Promotional emails and Webinars generated better conversion rates.

• Device-wise, mobile users convert more than desktop users, underlining the need for mobile-first campaign design.

On the flip side:

• Bounce and unsubscribe rates were slightly higher for mobile users and the “Lead” segment, highlighting a need for better audience segmentation and pre-send validation.

What this means for marketers:
• Segment smarter, personalized messaging to "Leads" could reduce churn.

• Prioritize mobile optimization; it is no longer optional.

• Double down on what works, push more campaigns with sign-up CTAs, and leverage high-performing regions.

• Track & refine,  every insight is a stepping stone for better ROI.
