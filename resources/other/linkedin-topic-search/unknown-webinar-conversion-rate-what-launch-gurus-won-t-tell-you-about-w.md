What 'launch gurus' won’t tell you about why your webinar conversion rates are dropping

Audience fatigue is real. 

People are tired of 90-minute webinars. 

Auctions create excitement and competition, which naturally boosts conversions without the "pitch" fatigue.

The problem: People are clicking away from webinars faster than ever.

They don't have time to waste listening to another sales pitch.

The insight: Auctions leverage "social proof" and "scarcity" in real-time.

When people see others bidding to solve a painful problem, they want to join in the fun.

Because only one person wins the auction prize, there is real scarcity.

The shift: Moving from "presenting" to "facilitating" a sale.

Auctions are much more simple to run.

You can do it from you phone.

You just make posts and reply to comments.

And reach out to the winner and bidders after the auction ends to collect payment from the winner and to make offers to the bidders.

This is a low stress, low skill, low tech way to bring in 5-6 figures in 7 days.
