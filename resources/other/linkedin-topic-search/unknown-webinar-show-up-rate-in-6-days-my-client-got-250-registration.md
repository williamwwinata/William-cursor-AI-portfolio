In 6 days my client got
- 250+ registration for a PAID webinar
- 71% show up rate
- 10% conversion rate
- 4X ROI

The best part was we recovered ad expenses by selling webinar tickets.

So whatever we sold on the webinar was profit.

This why I recommend paid webinars to my clients

→ You get a good quality audience because people who pay are serious.

→ You recover your ad expense by selling paid webinar tickets.

→ The show up rate and conversion rates are way better than free webinar.

I have helped different clients of mine do webinars both paid & free.

Irrespective of their niche, paid webinars have got a good audience.

If you are just starting out,

And you are doing your first ever webinars, then keeping it free is fine.

Use it to understand your audience, what they are looking for, their problems.

Once you get it, then do paid webinars to attract a good quality audience.
