Had an interesting conversation with a client yesterday that I thought you'd find valuable...

She asked me, "Uli, which lead magnet converts best?"

My answer surprised her.

Here's what our data shows:

PDF Guides:
✅ Highest opt-in rates (people love quick downloads!)
✅ Lower conversion to discovery calls
✅ Great for building your list

Video Series:
✅ Fewer opt-ins initially
✅ Higher conversion to discovery calls
✅ Better for building connection

Webinars:
✅ Lowest opt-in rates
✅ Highest conversion to discovery calls
✅ Excellent for complex topics

But here's where it gets really interesting... 🤔

When prospects consume BOTH a PDF guide AND a video series, their conversion rate to discovery calls actually exceeds webinar conversion rates!

Why? Because they're engaging with your content in different ways:

♥️ Reading (logical processing)
♥️ Watching (visual/emotional connection)
♥️ Multiple touchpoints (building trust)

The key takeaway? 🎯

Don't limit yourself to one format. Focus on creating content in various formats that work together to build trust and demonstrate your expertise.

Think of it like nutrition - a varied diet is always better than focusing on a single food group!
