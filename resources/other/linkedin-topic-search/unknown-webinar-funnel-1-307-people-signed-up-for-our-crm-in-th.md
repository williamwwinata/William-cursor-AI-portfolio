1,307 people signed up for our CRM in the last six months because someone invited them.

No ads.

No SDR outreach.

No webinar funnel.

Just one person telling another:

“You should try this.”

For a long time, we didn’t track it.

Invite-driven signups were buried inside “organic.”

But they behave very differently.

They activate faster.

They retain longer.

They invite others.

Because someone already explained the product before they signed up.

Word of mouth isn’t dead.

Most companies just aren’t measuring it.
