CTA like "Click here" or "Learn more" gets ignored. 

I use ultra-specific, benefit-laced microcopy that teases what’s on the other side.

2 examples:

1) “See how I tripled my sales with one funnel change.”

2) “Watch the 3-minute trick I used to 10x webinar show-up rates.”

It works because specificity increases perceived value, hence you’re not vague and you get more clicks.
