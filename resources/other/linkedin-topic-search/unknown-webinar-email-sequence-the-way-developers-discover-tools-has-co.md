The way developers discover tools has completely changed but the way most companies try to reach developers hasn't really caught up.

I am still seeing heavy investment in blog posts, webinars, email sequences while the actual discovery is happening in a discord thread, a reddit comment, a youtube video someone made for fun with no sponsorship.

Not saying the traditional stuff doesn't work at all but there's a gap between where teams are putting effort and where developers are actually paying attention and i think that gap is getting wider not smaller.

Would be curious what's actually working for people right now in terms of reaching developers authentically.
