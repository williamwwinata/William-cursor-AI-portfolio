Fathom's AI is pretty nice now. I had it analyze 241 Zoom call recordings in 39 seconds, asking it - what are the primary problems I'm solving on my trainings, hot seats, and workshops? 

It popped this out (I trimmed it way back, there was a lot more).
+ Poor webinar performance (low conversions or no live sales)
+ Low webinar show-up rates 
+ Weak offers / unclear pricing 
+ Bad lead quality or mismatched audiences 
+ Funnels that don’t convert or are overly complex 
+ Ad creative fatigue and rising CPLs 
+ Sales that depend on too many 1:1 calls (unscalable models) 
+ Clients stuck in “analysis paralysis” or marketing paralysis
+ Nervous-system barriers / overwhelm blocking execution 
+ Tech overload and manual ops 
+ Content that doesn’t convert (AI-sloppy copy or disorganized decks) 
+ Membership & community engagement problems 
+ Offer positioning / niche confusion 
+ Hiring, training, and support bottlenecks

I've had Fathom since August. That means an average of 26 calls a month, or 6 calls a week. I had no idea! 

Fathom is probably one of my best purchases of 2025. It renders recordings instantly, easily shareable, I have a Zap that sends the transcript to my email and then a Gemini flow in Gmail that summarizes the call to send to my clients, plus now with AI built into Fathom, I can harvest so much across ALL calls, not just an individual call. 

I'm not even sure what I pay for Fathom, but 100% worth it. Definitely an AI tool I say hell yes to (probably should be an affiliate huh). 

If you do a lot of calls and coaching, in my head - this is a no brainer.
