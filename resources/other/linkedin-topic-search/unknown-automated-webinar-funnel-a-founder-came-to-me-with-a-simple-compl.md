A founder came to me with a simple complaint,

“People love my content… but nobody buys.”

We changed it (in order):

Before:
– content created trust
– DMs carried the sales process
– calls were with cold people
– revenue spiked, then disappeared

We installed one bridge - an automated webinar funnel that did the explaining before the call.

After:
– fewer calls
– warmer calls
– faster decisions
– less emotional selling

The biggest win was that the business stopped feeling like a daily performance.
