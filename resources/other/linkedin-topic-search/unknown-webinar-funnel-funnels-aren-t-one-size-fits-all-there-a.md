Funnels aren't one-size-fits-all. There are 3 main types you should know:

1 Lead Generation Funnel - collects emails and builds relationships.
2 Sales Funnel - guides prospects to make a purchase.
3 Webinar / Value Funnel - delivers education first, then converts.

Pick the right funnel, guide your audience, and watch consistency turn into results.
