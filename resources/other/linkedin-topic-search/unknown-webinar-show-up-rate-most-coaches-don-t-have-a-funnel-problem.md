Most coaches don’t have a funnel problem.
They have a funnel clarity problem.

They build the funnel.
Run the ads.
Launch the program.
But when it doesn’t convert…
They don’t know why. 

Here’s the real issue:

👉 They’re not tracking the right metrics.
👉 Or worse, they’re not tracking anything at all.

You can’t optimize what you can’t measure.

Key numbers every coach should know: 

- Landing page conversion rate
- Cost per lead
- Webinar/show-up rate
- Sales call booking rate
- Cost per acquisition
- Funnel drop-off points

Without these, you’re just guessing.
And guessing is expensive.

✅ Funnel metrics are not about spreadsheets and stress.

They’re about clarity.

They tell you where people are dropping off and where you need to fix your messaging.

If you’re a coach and your funnel “is not working”…
Start with the numbers.

They’ll tell you what your audience is not saying.
