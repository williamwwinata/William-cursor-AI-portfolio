Amazon's Kindle Translate converts e-books between English-Spanish and German-English with one-click AI translation - free for indie authors versus typical $3,000-$10,000 human translator costs.

The system includes author approval gates, "Kindle Translate" badges for transparency, preview samples, and Kindle Unlimited eligibility while less than 5% of Amazon's catalog currently exists in multiple languages.
