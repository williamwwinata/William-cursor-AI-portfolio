Webinars aren’t dying - they are becoming AI-agents’ go-to source!


The rise of AI agents and generative search (GEO/LLM optimization) is reshaping content discovery. Traditional SEO targeted Google rankings; now, content must be structured, authoritative, and easy for models like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and agent runtimes to parse, summarize, and cite.

For B2B marketers, webinars remain powerful, but their value often stayed locked behind live attendance or gated replays. Much expertise disappeared if prospects didn’t engage deeply.

ON24’s recently released Discoverability feature—powered by the AI Analytics and Content Engine (ACE) and integrated into ON24 AI Propel+—changes that. It automatically generates AI-optimized assets from every webinar: dynamic FAQs, concise summaries, blog posts, eBooks, key takeaways, and more. These are structured outputs built for visibility in both classic search and emerging generative ecosystems.

Why It Matters in the Agent-Led Era  
- Ephemeral → Evergreen: One webinar fuels ongoing, discoverable content with proper headings, schema, and natural-language FAQs that LLMs love to cite.  
- GEO/LLM Visibility: Clear structure helps content appear in AI-generated answers to queries like “B2B pipeline best practices 2026.”  
- First-Party Flywheel: More discoverable assets drive organic traffic → deeper engagement on ON24 hubs → richer behavioral signals for personalization and scoring.

In an agent-augmented world where tools evaluate programmatically, brands win by becoming the most credible, machine-readable source. ON24 flips the script: turn webinars from temporary events into persistent thought leadership assets that agents, LLMs, and humans reference first.

This isn’t just repurposing—it’s strategic adaptation. Marketers embracing discoverability architect for the future, making every webinar the foundation of enduring influence.

Make your webinars AI-discoverable by converting events into always-on assets that fuel pipeline in the agent era.
