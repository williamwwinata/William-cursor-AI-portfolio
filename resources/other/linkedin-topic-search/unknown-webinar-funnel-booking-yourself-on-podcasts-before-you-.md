Booking yourself on podcasts before you even have one is like running ads to a website that doesn’t exist yet. 

Here’s what to do first:

1️⃣ Owned/Semi-owned Media (Podcast, Email, YT) 
2️⃣ Lead Magnet or Lead Quiz for Your Target Market 
3️⃣ Webinar / Call Funnel

Do this before you start doing PR.

Because getting people hyped without a place for them to go? 

That’s a big ole' waste of everybody's time.
