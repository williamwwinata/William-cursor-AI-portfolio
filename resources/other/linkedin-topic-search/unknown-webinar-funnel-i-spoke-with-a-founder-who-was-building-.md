I spoke with a founder who was building a SaaS for lawyers.
Their question: “Should I run ads or launch a webinar to attract them?”

Let's start from the beginning:
👉 “Lawyers” is not a target market. It’s a job titl. Surprise 😄

Corporate lawyers. Criminal defense lawyers. Solo practices. Large firms. Each has different workflows, pain points, and budgets.

Before you decide on webinars, ads, or funnels… you need to know:

 - Who exactly are you targeting?

 - What’s their #1 burning problem?

 - How do they already discover solutions like yours?

GTM isn’t about picking a channel. It’s about matching the right problem → message → market.
Once you know that, the right tactics become obvious.
