Joshua Hanosh said it out loud and I respect him for it: "The marketers or marketing services AI replaces were never that valuable to begin with."

That's going to sting for some people. It should.

This CMO Mindset newsletter is worth your ten minutes. Eric Johnson's line stopped me cold: you can't escalate a ticket to the model when something goes wrong. Somebody still has to own the outcome.

There used to be a saying: nobody gets fired for hiring IBM (this ages me... I know!). The logic was that a prestigious name covers you when things go sideways. AI doesn't work that way. "The model did it" is not a defense. It's a confession.

Bravo gentlemen!
