I hate to bring the bad news: nobody watches those old, hour-long webinar recordings.

It’s just human nature. We’re not wired—especially now—to watch a previously-live video for an entire hour. Every second, the temptation to pause is present. Eventually, viewers tire out, plan to finish it later, and never return.

Worse, marketers will use the on-demand webinar recording to justify the webinar’s value, despite lackluster attendance. It’s a vicious cycle. But it doesn’t need to be this way.

Don’t post your webinar recording. The full session is only for the attendees. They earned in. For everyone else, post snippets instead. TikTok has confirmed that the short form-factor is something that modern day humans crave. Use that length, while applying the professionalism of your brand, to create recurring engagement across your social channels. The best part? You only need to showcase the best parts that’ll provide your future customers with evergreen value.

Webinar → Snippets → Socials is a content marketing strategy that works. Abandon that long Wistia or YouTube embed and embrace the short-form content!
